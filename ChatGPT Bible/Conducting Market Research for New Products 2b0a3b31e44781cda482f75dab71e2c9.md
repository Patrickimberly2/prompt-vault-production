# Conducting Market Research for New Products

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
I want to introduce a product in **[specific industry/market]**. 
Suggest market research techniques to understand customer needs 
and preferences.
```

```jsx
My target audience is **[specific demographic]**. Write a survey or 
interview script to gather insights about their challenges and 
desires.
```

```jsx
I need to analyze competitors in **[specific market]**. Provide a plan 
for researching their strengths, weaknesses, and market strategies.
```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

- What are the most effective methods for conducting market research on a budget?
- How do I identify unmet needs or gaps in the market?
- What tools can I use to collect and analyze market data effectively?
- How do I segment my audience for more targeted market research?
- What are the best practices for conducting competitor analysis?
- How do I validate product ideas using primary research (e.g., surveys, focus groups)?
- What role does secondary research play in understanding market trends?
- How can I use social media to gather real-time customer insights?
- What metrics should I track to determine if a new product idea has potential?
- How do I prioritize feedback from market research when developing a product?

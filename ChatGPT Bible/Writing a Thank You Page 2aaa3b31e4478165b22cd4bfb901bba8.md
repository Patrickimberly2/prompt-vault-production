# Writing a Thank You Page

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
I want to write the copy for the thank you page of my [**type of product**] called [**name of the product**]. In this [**type of product**] buyers [**outcome**]. Can you write me the copy for the thank you page? Use a [**adjective**] tone of voice. Mention [**things you want to mention**].
```

```jsx
I have a [**type of product**] that helps [**target audience**] [**outcome**]. Write the copy for the thank you page. Make it under [**number of words**]. Use [**benefit**].
```

```jsx
Here is the copy I wrote for the sales page of my [**product**]: "[**insert some paragraphs of the sales page copy**].
Write the thank you page copy. Make it aligned to the sales page copy and send people [**page or piece of content you want to send them**].
```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "What are some tips for creating an effective thank you page after someone signs up for my course?"
2. "How can I make my thank you page more engaging and effective at converting leads into customers?"
3. "What are the essential elements that should be included in a thank you page for a course or product?"
4. "How can I use the thank you page to encourage customers to take further action or engage with my brand?"
5. "What is the best way to convey appreciation to customers on a thank you page?"
6. "What are some examples of effective thank you pages that I can model for my own course or product?"
7. "How can I use storytelling on the thank you page to connect with customers and build trust?"
8. "What should the tone and style of my thank you page be to resonate with my target audience?"
9. "What is the ideal length for a thank you page, and how can I keep it concise while still conveying the necessary information?"
10. "What are some common mistakes to avoid when creating a thank you page, and how can I ensure that mine is effective?"

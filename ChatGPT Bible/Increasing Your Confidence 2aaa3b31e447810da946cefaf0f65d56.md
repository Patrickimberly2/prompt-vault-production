# Increasing Your Confidence

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
You are a confident business owner in the **[industry]**. What are the best ways **[industry]** business owners can boost condifence when **[situation]**?
```

```jsx
Pretend you are a therapist. I am a business owner and I feel **[your feeling]** when I have to **[situation]**. How can I boost my confidence in that kind of situation?
```

```jsx
What do **[your target audience]** see as insecure? And how can I prevent that?
```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "What are some techniques for maintaining a positive attitude and projecting confidence in the face of criticism or negative feedback from the public or media?"
2. "How can I leverage social media and other online platforms to build my personal brand and increase my confidence as a public figure?"
3. "Can you provide me with some examples of successful public relations campaigns or initiatives, and help me identify the key factors that made them successful?"
4. "What are some techniques for developing effective talking points and messaging that can help me stay on message and project confidence in interviews or public appearances?"
5. "How can I prepare myself for difficult or confrontational interviews, so that I can feel more confident and in control?"
6. "Can you provide me with some techniques for effective public speaking, such as body language, eye contact, and voice projection?"
7. "What are some ways to build relationships with journalists and media professionals, so that I can feel more confident and connected within the industry?"
8. "Can you help me identify my unique strengths and expertise, and help me leverage them to build my confidence and credibility as a thought leader or spokesperson?"
9. "What are some effective ways to handle negative publicity or crisis situations, so that I can maintain my composure and confidence while addressing the situation?"
10. "Can you provide me with some resources or training programs that can help me build my confidence and expertise in public relations, such as networking events, industry conferences, or mentorship opportunities?"

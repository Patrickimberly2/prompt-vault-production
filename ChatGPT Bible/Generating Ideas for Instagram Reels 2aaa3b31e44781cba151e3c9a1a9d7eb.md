# Generating Ideas for Instagram Reels

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
My business sells courses on how to [**what your course teaches**]. What are some creative ideas for Instagram reels that would go viral?
```

```jsx
What is an Instagram reel idea the majority of [**niche**] coaches are overlooking?
```

```jsx
Can you give me ideas for an Instagram reel for a [**niche**] coach, maybe on topics like [**insert 3 topics you are an expert on**].
```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. “What are some creative themes or concepts that I can use for my Instagram reels?”
2. “Can you suggest some fun and engaging props or accessories that I can incorporate into my Instagram reels?”
3. “How can I leverage trending topics or challenges to create unique and relevant Instagram reels?”
4. “What are some ways I can use humor or satire to make my Instagram reels more entertaining and shareable?”
5. “Can you recommend some Instagram accounts or hashtags that I can follow for inspiration and ideas?”
6. “How can I repurpose existing content or ideas for my Instagram reels without making them feel repetitive or boring?”
7. “What are some storytelling techniques that I can use to make my Instagram reels more engaging and compelling?”
8. “Can you suggest some music tracks or sound effects that can enhance the mood or message of my Instagram reels?”
9. “What are some creative ways I can use special effects or filters to make my Instagram reels stand out?”
10. “How can I involve my audience in the creation of my Instagram reels to increase engagement and build a community around my content?”

# Creating New Offers

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
My brand's core business is **[type of product]**.
I want to create a new offer. Can you give me effective ideas that could work in the **[industry]**?
```

```jsx
I want to create a new offer for the **[market]**.
My goal is to help **[target audience] [outcome]**. How can I structure my new offer?
```

```jsx
Can you give me effective idea for a **[niche]** new offer for **[target audience]**?
```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "How can I create innovative and compelling offers that will appeal to my target market?"
2. "Would it be possible for you to help me develop a strategy for launching new offers for my business?"
3. "Can you provide examples of successful new offers that have been launched in my industry?"
4. "What are the key factors to consider when developing new offers for my business?"
5. "Can you help me conduct market research to identify new opportunities for my business?"
6. "How can I ensure that my new offers align with my business goals and values?"
7. "What are some effective marketing strategies for promoting new offers to potential customers?"
8. "Can you provide me with guidance on pricing my new offers competitively?"
9. "What should I keep in mind when testing and refining my new offers before launching them to the market?"
10. 1. "Can you assist me in brainstorming new product or service ideas for my business?"

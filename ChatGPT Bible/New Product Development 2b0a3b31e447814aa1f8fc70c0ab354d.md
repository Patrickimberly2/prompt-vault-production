```

```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

- What are the key steps in the new product development process?
- How do I conduct market research to validate product ideas?
- What tools or methods can I use to create and test prototypes?
- How do I prioritize product features based on customer needs and feedback?
- What are the best practices for launching a new product in a competitive market?
- How do I align product development with my overall business goals?
- What role does iterative testing play in refining new products?
- How can I measure the success of a new product launch?
- What are the most effective ways to involve customers in the product development process?
- How do I identify and mitigate risks associated with new product development?

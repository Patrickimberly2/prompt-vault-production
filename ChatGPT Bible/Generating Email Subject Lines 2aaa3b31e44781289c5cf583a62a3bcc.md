# Generating Email Subject Lines

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
Write 3 subject line alternatives I can use for my email about [**topic of the email**]. The main goal is to get people to open the email.
```

```jsx
Here is the email I wrote: [**insert email copy**]. Write me 5 potential subject lines that would get in the Primary tab, stand out in the inbox and get open. Use 10 words tops.
```

```jsx
I want to write an effective subject line for my email marketing that I'll send to [**target audience**]. What should I look for in my email to come up with an attention-grabbing subject line?
```

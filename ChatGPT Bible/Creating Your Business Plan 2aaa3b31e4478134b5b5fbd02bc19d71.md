# Creating Your Business Plan

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
Give me a range of products or services that I could provide within **[business area].** 
Detail how they will distinguish themselves from competitors.
```

```jsx
Write a business plan for my business called **[name of the company]** that wants to reach **[goal],** and **s**ells **[product/ service]** to **[target audience].** 
```

```jsx
Write a simple guide to organize my business in **[industry],** and include recommendations for tools I can use to structure my **[finances/operation/marketing]**.
```

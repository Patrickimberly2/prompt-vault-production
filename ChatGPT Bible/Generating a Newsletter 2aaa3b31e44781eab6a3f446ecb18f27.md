# Generating a Newsletter

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
I am a [**niche**] coach. I want to create a weekly email newsletter about [**main topic you want to cover**]. Can you help me write the emails?
```

```jsx
I need new ideas for my weekly email newsletter about [**topics you cover**]. Can you give me 10 new ideas I can develop? I am a [**niche**] coach for [**audience**].
```

```jsx
I write a weekly email newsletter about [**main topic**]. Here is one I wrote: [**add your newsletter**].
Can you help me write a new email about [**topic you want to cover**]?
```

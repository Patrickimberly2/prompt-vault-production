# Increasing Customer Retention

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
I am a [**coach/consultant**] for [**audience**]. Give me 10 ideas on how I can increase customer retention of my [**product**].
```

```jsx
In my [**type of product**] I help people [**outcome**], but after [**amount of time**] they stop renewing. Give me some strategies I can use to make them renew for a longer period of time.
```

```jsx
I get many clients through my front-end offer about [**topic**]. It helps [**audience**] [**get outcome**]. Can you give me ideas on new products I can promote to them after that so that I increase customer retention?
```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "What are some effective strategies to improve customer retention in my coaching and consulting business?"
2. "How can I keep my clients engaged and motivated to continue working with me?"
3. "What can I do to add more value to my services and make my clients feel like they're getting the most out of their investment?"
4. "What are some ways I can personalize my approach to client retention based on individual needs and preferences?"
5. "How can I use technology and automation to enhance the customer experience and increase retention rates?"
6. "What are some creative ways to incentivize clients to continue working with me?"
7. "How can I incorporate feedback from clients to improve my services and increase retention?"
8. "What role does community and social support play in retaining clients and how can I leverage this in my business?"
9. "How can I build a strong brand identity and reputation that fosters long-term relationships with clients?"
10. "What are some common mistakes that coaches and consultants make that lead to poor customer retention and how can I avoid them?"

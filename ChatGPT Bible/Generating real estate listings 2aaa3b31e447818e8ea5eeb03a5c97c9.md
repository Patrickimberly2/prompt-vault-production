# Generating real estate listings

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
How can i enhance my real estate listing for a **[property type]** in **[location]**? Please provide **[adjectives]** to describe the **[property features]** and **[adjectives]** to describe the **[location]**.
```

```jsx
Can you assist me in creating a real estate listing for a **[property type]** in **[location]** that captures the attention of **[potential buyers]**? I would appreciate your help in researching **[property features]** and **[nearby amenities]** that are important to highlight.
```

```jsx
How can i enhance my real estate listing for a **[property type]** in **[location]**, considering the **[property]** is **[current condition]**?
```

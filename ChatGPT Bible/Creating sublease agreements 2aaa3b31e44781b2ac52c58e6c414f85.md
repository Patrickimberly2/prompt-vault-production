### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "Why is it important to create a well-drafted sublease agreement when allowing a tenant to sublet a rental property? How does it protect the interests of both the original tenant and the subtenant?"
2. "In what ways can a sublease agreement clearly outline the terms and conditions under which the sublease is granted, including the duration, rent payment details, and any restrictions or obligations?"
3. "How does a sublease agreement establish the legal relationship between the original tenant, subtenant, and landlord, ensuring that all parties understand their rights and responsibilities?"
4. "Why is it crucial for a sublease agreement to include provisions regarding the consent of the landlord, obtaining necessary approvals, and complying with any specific requirements outlined in the original lease?"
5. "What role does a sublease agreement play in addressing liability and indemnification, protecting the original tenant from any damages, unpaid rent, or other issues caused by the subtenant?"
6. "How can a sublease agreement address the transfer of utilities, maintenance responsibilities, and property access arrangements between the original tenant and subtenant?"
7. "What measures can be included in a sublease agreement to address any specific rules or restrictions imposed by the landlord, such as pet policies, smoking prohibitions, or limitations on use?"
8. "Why is it important for a sublease agreement to clearly state the consequences of any breach or violation of the agreement, including potential termination of the sublease or legal actions?"
9. "In what ways can a sublease agreement outline the procedures for resolving disputes between the original tenant and subtenant, promoting effective communication and conflict resolution?"
10. "What steps can landlords take to ensure that the sublease agreement complies with local laws and regulations, protecting the interests of all parties and maintaining the integrity of the rental property?"

# Building Your Sales Script

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
I want to write a sales script to turn my [**lead source**] leads into clients for my [**niche**] coaching business. What is a good process I can use?
```

```jsx
I am a [**niche**] coach and I help [**target audience**] [**outcome**]. Their biggest isse at the moment is [**problem**]. Can you write an effective sales script? Mention [**concepts you want to mention**].
```

```jsx
Here is the draft of a sales script I use to turn [**target audience**] into coaching clients: "[**insert script**]".
Can you help me improve and optimize it?
```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "What steps should I follow to create a sales script that converts leads into clients for my coaching business?"
2. "How can I identify the pain points of my target audience and use them to craft a compelling sales script for my coaching services?"
3. "What are some effective techniques for establishing credibility and demonstrating the value of my coaching services in a sales script?"
4. "How can I use social proof to build trust and persuade potential clients to invest in my coaching services?"
5. "What types of questions should I ask in a sales script to encourage prospects to share their goals and challenges, and how can I use this information to customize my pitch?"
6. "What are some effective strategies for overcoming objections in a sales script and addressing concerns about cost, time commitment, or other competing priorities?"
7. "How can I close the sale in a sales script and encourage prospects to commit to working with me?"
8. "What are some best practices for follow-up messaging after a sales call, and how can I use these to keep prospects engaged and motivated?"
9. "What language should I use in a sales script to create a sense of urgency and motivate prospects to take action now rather than later?"
10. "What are some common mistakes that coaches make when creating sales scripts, and how can I avoid them?"

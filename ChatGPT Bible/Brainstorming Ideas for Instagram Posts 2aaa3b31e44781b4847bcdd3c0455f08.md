# Brainstorming Ideas for Instagram Posts

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
You are an A-list social media manager. Tell me how I can come up with ideas for Instagram posts fast for my consulting business in the [**industry**].
```

```jsx
How can I brainstorm new and fresh Instagram post ideas for my [**coaching/consulting**] business?
```

```jsx
My consulting business helps [**target audience**] [**reach goal**]. What are some good ideas for Instagram posts that highlight my expertise?
```

1. "How can I come up with new and creative ideas for my Instagram posts?"

# Writing a Cold Email

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
You are an A-list cold email expert. I am a [**niche**] coach. Write me a cold email for [**brief description of the person/company**].
```

```jsx
Give me an effective cold email template that [**niche**] [**coaches/consultants**] can use to [**goal**]. Make it a cold email they have never seen.
```

```jsx
What are the most effective cold email strategies that [**niche**] [**coaches/consultants**] can use when reaching out to [**type of people**]?
```

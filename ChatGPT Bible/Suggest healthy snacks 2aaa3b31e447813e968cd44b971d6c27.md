# Suggest healthy snacks

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
What are some nutritious snack options that are **[low/high]** in **[protein/fiber], [low/high]** in **[calories/sugar]**, and suitable for **[vegans/vegetarians/athletes/diabetics]**?
```

```jsx
Can you provide recommendations for **[easy/quick]** snack options that are **[low/high]** in **[calories/protein/fiber]**?
```

```jsx
Could you suggest **[nutritious/energy-boosting]** snacks that are appropriate for **[kids/adults/elderly]** with **[lactose intolerance/certain allergies/other specific dietary requirements]**?
```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "What are some delicious and nutritious snack options that can satisfy my cravings?"
2. "Can you recommend some quick and easy-to-prepare snacks that are both healthy and filling?"
3. "What are some low-calorie snack alternatives that can help with weight management?"
4. "Are there any healthy snack options that can provide an energy boost during the day?"
5. "Can you suggest some protein-rich snacks that are suitable for post-workout recovery?"
6. "What are some healthy snack choices for people with dietary restrictions, such as gluten-free or vegan options?"
7. "Are there any snack ideas that can help curb sugar cravings in a healthy way?"
8. "Can you provide some ideas for nutritious snacks that are kid-friendly and appealing to picky eaters?"
9. "What are some snack options that promote brain health and enhance cognitive function?"
10. "Are there any creative snack ideas that incorporate fruits and vegetables to increase nutrient intake?"

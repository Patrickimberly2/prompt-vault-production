3. "How can I ensure that my team members are managing their stress levels during stressful work situations?"
4. "What are some ways to prevent burnout in myself and my team when we are dealing with a lot of stress?"
5. "What strategies can I use to promote a positive and supportive work environment during stressful times?"
6. "How can I prioritize my tasks and manage my workload to reduce stress in the workplace?"
7. "What are some effective ways to communicate with my team during a stressful situation?"
8. "How can I help my team members to develop their stress-management skills?"
9. "What resources are available to me and my team to help manage stress in the workplace?"
10. "What steps can I take to proactively reduce stress and prevent stressful situations from arising within the work environment?"

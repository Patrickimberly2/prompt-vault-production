# Creating Proposals

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
I just had a call with a potential client who is having **[problem]** and wants to **[outcome]**.
My way to help **[him/her]** is: **[brief description of your offer, products, services and pricing]**.
Write an effective proposal for my client.
```

```jsx
I run a business that sells **[product or service]**.
How can I package my **[products or services]** in a way that **[target audience]** want to work with me long-term?
```

```jsx
I have a **[type of business]** business and I sell **[product or service]** that **[outcome]**. I send proposals after a sales call.
How can I structure the proposal in a way that turns leads into clients?
```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "What are the key elements that should be included in a sales proposal to make it effective?"
2. "Can you provide some guidance on how to structure a sales proposal for maximum impact?"
3. "What are some effective techniques for personalizing a sales proposal for a specific prospect or customer?"
4. "What's the ideal length and level of detail for a sales proposal, and how can I strike the right balance?"
5. "What are some common mistakes to avoid when creating a sales proposal?"
6. "Can you recommend any tools or software that can help streamline the process of creating sales proposals?"
7. "How can I use visual aids such as graphs and charts to enhance the effectiveness of my sales proposals?"
8. "What's the best way to incorporate customer feedback and testimonials into a sales proposal?"
9. "How can I use my sales proposal as a tool for building rapport and establishing trust with prospects?"
10. "What's the best way to follow up on a sales proposal and move the prospect closer to a buying decision?"

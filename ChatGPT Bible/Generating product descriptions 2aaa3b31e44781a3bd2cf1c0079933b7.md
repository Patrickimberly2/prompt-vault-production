# Generating product descriptions

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
Please offer a product description of **[product name]**. This **[product category]** is distinguished by its **[main feature/benefit]**. It has been specifically crafted for **[target audience]** and boasts **[product specification]**.
```

```jsx
I require a product description for **[product name]**, which belongs to the **[product category]**. Renowned for its **[main feature/benefit]**, this product is tailored for **[target audience]** and boasts **[product specification]**, along with an added **[additional feature/benefit]**.
```

```jsx
Could you compose a product description for **[product name]**? This **[product category]** caters perfectly to **[target audience]** seeking to **[main feature/benefit]**. With **[product specification]** and **[additional feature/benefit]**, it also showcases a distinctive selling point of **[unique selling point]**.
```

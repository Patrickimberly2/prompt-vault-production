# Researching Ideas for Podcasts

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
Can you give me ideas for a podcast episode for a [**niche**] coach, maybe on topics like [**insert 3 topics you are an expert on**].
```

```jsx
My business sells courses on how to [**what your course teaches**]. What are some creative ideas for podcast episodes that would attract [**target audience**] and make them listen?
```

```jsx
Can you give me 10 podcast ideas a [**target audience**] looking for [**topics**] would listen to?
```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "What are some effective brainstorming techniques for generating podcast episode ideas?"
2. "How can I tap into my creativity to come up with unique and interesting topics for my podcast?"
3. "What resources can I use to research and identify trends or popular topics that would make for a successful podcast episode?"
4. "How can I use feedback from my listeners to inform my next podcast episode topic?"
5. "What are some strategies for finding and inviting engaging guests who can speak to relevant and compelling topics?"
6. "How can I identify gaps in my industry or niche that I could fill with a thought-provoking podcast episode?"
7. "What are some ways to add a unique spin to a popular topic or trend, to make it stand out in my podcast?"
8. "How can I keep my podcast episode topics fresh and interesting, to keep listeners engaged and coming back for more?"
9. "What role does my personal experiences and expertise play in generating podcast episode ideas, and how can I leverage that to my advantage?"
10. "What are some of the most successful podcast episode topics that have resonated with audiences, and how can I adapt those topics to suit my own podcast?"

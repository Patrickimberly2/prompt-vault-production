# Generating product manuals

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
Please generate a product manual for **[product name]**, encompassing instructions on product usage, troubleshooting advice, and pertinent safety guidelines.
```

```jsx
I require a printable booklet-style product manual for **[product name]**. The manual should be structured into chapters, incorporate diagrams and photographs, and feature a table of contents.

```

```jsx
I'm in need of a product manual for **[product name]** that adopts a conversational style and is easily comprehensible for individuals without technical expertise. It should contain straightforward, step-by-step instructions along with a section dedicated to frequently asked questions (FAQs).
```

# Communicating Your Vision

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
I am a [**coach/consultant**] for [**audience**]. I have a team of [**number of team members**]. My vision is: [**brief description of the vision you have for your company**]. Can you give me ideas on how I can communicate it effectively? I want my team to feel part of this vision.
```

```jsx
Give me a step-by-step guide on how I as the CEO of my [**niche**] [**coaching/consulting**] business can communicate my vision to my team in a way that makes them want to be part of it.
```

```jsx
I run a [**coaching/consulting**] business where I help [**target audience**] [**outcome**]. In my team I work with [**people in house/freelancers/both**]. How can I keep my vision top of mind within my team? I meet my team members [**medium you use**] [**what you do**].
```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "What are some effective ways to communicate my vision to my team?"
2. "How can I ensure that my team fully understands and supports my vision?"
3. "What communication strategies can I use to keep my team focused on our shared vision?"
4. "What tools or resources can I use to visually communicate my vision to my team?"
5. "How can I make sure that my team members are aligned with our vision and working towards our goals?"
6. "What are some common communication pitfalls to avoid when sharing my vision with my team?"
7. "How often should I communicate my vision with my team, and in what format?"
8. "What role does active listening play in effectively communicating my vision with my team?"
9. "What are some ways to encourage feedback and discussion around our shared vision within my team?"
10. "How can I create a sense of ownership and investment in our shared vision among my team members?"

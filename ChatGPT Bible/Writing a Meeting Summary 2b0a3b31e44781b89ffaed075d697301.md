# Writing a Meeting Summary

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
Summarize the 5 main takeaways from these meeting notes: **[copy and paste notes].**
```

```jsx
Summarize the decisions made and the next steps outlined in these meeting notes: **[copy and paste notes].**
```

```jsx
Summarize the key points raised, the solutions proposed and the responsible departments in these meeting notes: **[copy and paste notes].**
```

### OPEN-ENDED **PROMPTS:**

1. “Can you please summarize the main points discussed in this meeting today? [[copy and paste transcript of the meeting].”
2. “I would appreciate it if you could provide a brief summary of this meeting we just had. [copy and paste transcript of the meeting].”
3. “Could you write up a summary of this meeting we had earlier? I want to make sure I didn't miss anything important: [copy and paste transcript of the meeting].”
4. “Please summarize the key takeaways from this meeting: [copy and paste transcript of the meeting].”
5. “Could you put together a meeting summary that I can share with other stakeholders? Here is the transcript of the recordings: [copy and paste transcript of the meeting].”
6. “I need to send a follow-up email to everyone who attended this meeting [copy and paste the transcript of the recordings]. Can you write a summary that I can include in the email?”

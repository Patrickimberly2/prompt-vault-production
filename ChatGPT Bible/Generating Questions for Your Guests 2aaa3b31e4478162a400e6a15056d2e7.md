# Generating Questions for Your Guests

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
I will interview a [**short description about your guest**]. Can you help me come up with topics we can cover during our interview?
```

```jsx
Give me 10 questions I can ask to a [**short description of your podcast guest**] for my podcast episode. Make them [**adjectives**].
```

```jsx
My podcast is about [**topics**]. The tone is [**adjectives**]. My next guest is a [**short description of the guest**]. Give me the 5 best questions I could ask [**him/her**].
```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "Can you share some tips on how to come up with interesting questions for my upcoming podcast episode?"
2. "What are some strategies for generating thought-provoking questions for a podcast interview?"
3. "I'm struggling to brainstorm questions for my podcast guest, do you have any advice?"
4. "How can I avoid asking the same generic questions that everyone else asks in a podcast interview?"
5. "What are some creative ways to approach question formulation for a podcast episode?"
6. "Do you have any resources or tools that can help me generate unique questions for my podcast?"
7. "How can I tailor my questions to suit the specific interests and expertise of my podcast guest?"
8. "What are some effective techniques for conducting research to inform my podcast questions?"
9. "How can I balance preparation with spontaneity when formulating my podcast questions?"
10. "Do you have any examples of successful podcast hosts who excel at asking engaging and insightful questions?"

# Preparing Your Curriculum

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
I am a [**niche**] coach for [**target audience**]. I am creating a high-ticket program that [**what the program helps with**]. I want to give [**insert type of coaching, like live sessions/webinars/videos/1-1 feedback/etc.**]. How can I structure my curriculum?
```

```jsx
As a [**niche**] course creator I am creating a high-ticket program that helps [**audience**] [**outcome**]: what topics and resources should I include in my course so that it's comprehensive and effective?
```

```jsx
I work as a [**coach/consultant**] in the [**industry**]. I am creating a new high-ticket program and I want to help [**audience**] with [**insert 3 problems you want to help with**]. What modules and resources should I include in my program?
```

4. "What resources and tools can I use to develop a comprehensive curriculum for my high-ticket coaching program?"
5. "What are the essential components that must be included in a high-ticket coaching program, and how can I incorporate them into my curriculum?"
6. "What strategies can I use to ensure that my high-ticket coaching program provides tangible results for my clients?"
7. "How can I create a high-ticket coaching program that caters to different learning styles and preferences?"
8. "What are the best practices for designing and delivering high-ticket coaching programs that are both effective and engaging?"
9. "Can you provide me with some examples of successful high-ticket coaching programs and their key features?"
10. "How can I create a high-ticket coaching program that stands out in a crowded market and attracts my ideal clients?"

# Writing diversity and inclusion job descriptions

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
Could you assist me in crafting an inclusive job description for a role at **[company name]**, emphasizing our dedication to diversity and inclusion while appealing to a wide range of candidates? We are particularly interested in candidates with **[adjective]** qualities, such as **[skill or qualification]**, **[skill or qualification],** and **[skill or qualification]**.
```

```jsx
In what ways can I enhance the attractiveness of my job posting to engage candidates from a diverse background for a **[position title]** role at **[company name]**, while ensuring the language used is inclusive and unbiased? We seek individuals with **[adjectives]** skills and experience in **[skill or qualification]** and **[skill or qualification]**, who can effectively **[responsibility or duty]**.
```

```jsx
What are some examples of inclusive language, characterized by **[adjectives]**, that I can incorporate into a job posting for a **[position title]** role at **[company name]**, effectively showcasing our dedication to diversity and inclusion? Our objective is to emphasize our encouragement of applications from **[diverse group]** candidates and underscore the significance of **[value or principle]** within our workplace culture. We are seeking individuals with experience in **[skill or qualification]** and **[skill or qualification]**, who can proficiently fulfill **[responsibility or duty].**
```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. Why is it important to incorporate diversity and inclusion elements into job descriptions?"
2. "What language and terminology should be used to promote diversity and inclusion in job descriptions?"
3. "How can job descriptions be tailored to attract candidates from diverse backgrounds and underrepresented groups?"
4. "Why is it crucial to highlight the organization's commitment to diversity, equity, and inclusion within the job description?"
5. "What specific qualifications or experiences should be emphasized to attract candidates who value diversity and inclusion?"
6. "How can job descriptions showcase the organization's efforts to create an inclusive work environment?"
7. "What role does unconscious bias play in writing job descriptions, and how can it be mitigated?"
8. "Why is it important to include diversity and inclusion as key criteria for evaluating candidates in the job description?"
9. "What strategies can be used to ensure the language and requirements in job descriptions do not inadvertently exclude certain groups?"
10. "How can job descriptions be continuously reviewed and improved to promote diversity, equity, and inclusion in the hiring process?"

# Pitching Investors

```jsx
I'm pitching an investor for **[product/service] that helps [target audience]** to **[describes outcomes].** Our business plan includes **[describe strategies from your 
business plan].**

Improve this idea by explaining the benefits for **[target audience].**

```

```jsx
Give me 5 keys to identify and emphasize the unique selling point of my **[industry]** business,**[company name]**, in my investor pitch. 
```

```jsx
Provide a list of effective templates for presenting financial projections to long-term investors in **[industry]** industry. 
```

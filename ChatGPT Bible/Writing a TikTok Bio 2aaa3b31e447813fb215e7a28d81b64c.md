# Writing a TikTok Bio

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
I am a [**niche**] consultant and I sell courses that help people [**what your students/clients get**]. Write me an effective bio for my TikTok account.
```

```jsx
My content talks about [**topics you cover in your content**]. Can you write 3 bio alternatives I can use for my TikTok bio? Make it under 80 characters.
```

```jsx
How can I write my TikTok bio as a [**niche**] [**coach/consultant/course creator**]? I want it to [**goal you want to reach through your TikTok bio**].
```

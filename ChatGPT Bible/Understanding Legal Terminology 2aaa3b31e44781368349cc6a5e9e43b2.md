# Understanding Legal Terminology

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
You are an expert lawyer. Explain in a simple way what **"[legal term]**" means.
```

```jsx
I found **[legal term/phrase]** in my contract. What does it mean?
```

```jsx
I have to sign a **[type of contract]** contract. Can you give me a list of legal terms with that **[type of of contract]** contracts contain? Include their explanation in simple language.
```

1. "Can you provide me with a list of common legal terms I should know for my business?"
2. "What is the difference between a contract and a memorandum of understanding?"
3. "Can you explain the meaning of the term 'indemnification' in a legal context?"
4. "How do I differentiate between 'breach of contract' and 'breach of warranty'?"
5. "What is the significance of 'force majeure' in a legal agreement?"
6. "Can you clarify the difference between 'liquidated damages' and 'penalties' in a legal contract?"
7. "What does 'remedies at law' and 'equitable remedies' mean in a legal context?"
8. "How is 'assignment' different from 'delegation' in a legal agreement?"
9. "What is the meaning of 'statute of limitations' in legal terminology?"
10. "Can you explain the concept of 'discovery' in legal proceedings?"

# Writing property performance reports

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
Evaluate the **[performance metric]** of **[property name]** throughout the **[time frame]**, while uncovering valuable insights regarding the trends and patterns within the data. Pinpointing influential factors, including alterations in **[market factors]** or distinctive **[property features]**, that may have impacted the performance.
```

```jsx
Conduct an analysis to identify the primary challenges faced by **[property name]**, focusing on **[staffing challenges]**, **[guest experience]**, and **[operational efficiencies]**. Present actionable recommendations to effectively address these challenges, leading to improved outcomes in terms of staff management, guest satisfaction, and operational effectiveness.
```

```jsx
Conduct a comprehensive assessment of the **[market segment]** in which **[property name]** operates to evaluate the prevailing **[market trend]**. Based on the analysis, offer actionable recommendations to adapt the current **[strategy]** and align it effectively with the market conditions. The assessment should consider key factors, including **[demand trends]**, **[supply factors]**, and **[competitor strategies]**. The expected outcome of this evaluation is a set of informed adjustments to the strategy that will enhance the property's competitiveness and optimize its performance within the market segment.
```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "What were the key factors driving the overall performance of the property during the reporting period?"
2. "How did the property's occupancy rate compare to the market average, and what factors contributed to this performance?"
3. "What were the notable trends in rental prices or sales prices for the property, and how did they compare to the broader market?"
4. "Did any specific property features or amenities have a significant impact on the property's performance? If so, what were they?"
5. "Were there any notable changes in the local market conditions or economic factors that affected the property's performance?"
6. "How did the property's financial performance, such as rental income or net operating income, compare to the previous reporting period or similar properties in the area?"
7. "What were the major challenges or obstacles that the property faced during the reporting period, and how were they addressed?"
8. "What were the main drivers of guest satisfaction or dissatisfaction, and what strategies were implemented to improve the guest experience?"
9. "Did the property implement any cost-saving measures or operational efficiencies that positively impacted its financial performance? If so, what were they?"
10. "What recommendations can be made to optimize a property's performance?"

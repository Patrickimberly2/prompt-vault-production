# Picking a Niche

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
I have good coaching skills and I am passionate about [**what you are passionate about**]: what are the best niches I should work in?
```

```jsx
Give me the best subniches in the [**niche**] coaching industry.
```

```jsx
I work as a [**coach/consultant**] in the [**industry**], but I want to choose a specific niche. What things should I look for?
```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "What factors should I consider when choosing a niche as a coach or consultant?"
2. "How can I identify a profitable niche that aligns with my interests and expertise?"
3. "What are some popular niches in the coaching and consulting industry that I can consider?"
4. "Can you give me some tips on how to narrow down my niche options?"
5. "How do I determine if there is a market demand for the niche I am considering?"
6. "What are some potential challenges I should be aware of when choosing a coaching niche?"
7. "How do I differentiate myself from other coaches or consultants in my chosen niche?"
8. "What resources are available to help me research potential niches and evaluate their profitability?"
9. "Can you provide me with some examples of successful coaches or consultants in different niches?"
10. "What steps can I take to test and validate my niche before committing to it?"
11. “What are the best methods to find the best coaching niche?”
12. “How do I know if a niche is already too saturated?”

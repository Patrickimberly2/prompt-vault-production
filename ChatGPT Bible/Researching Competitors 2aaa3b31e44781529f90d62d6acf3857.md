# Researching Competitors

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
Provide a raking from best to worst of the following competitors in **[business area]**  based on **[pricing/customer service/ market position]** in **[country/city].** 

Include an explanation for why each one has been ranked in this order. 

**- [competitor 1]
- [competitor 2]
- [competitor 3]
- [competitor 4]**
```

```jsx
Create a comparative analysis of strengths, weaknesses, brand position and sales performance of my competitors in **[business area], specifically [competitor 1], 
[competitor 2], [competitor 3]**, and **[competitor 4].**
```

```jsx
Give me a step-by-step guide on how I can find the best marketing strategies my competitors in the **[niche]** are using with their customers.
```

4. "Can you gather data and information on my competition in [industry] ****and provide me with an overview?"
5. "Could you please investigate [industry] competitors' strengths and weaknesses?"
6. "I want to understand my competitors in  [industry] better. Can you do some research and provide me with a competitive landscape?"
7. "I need to know more about my competitors' products and services. Could you help me with some research?"
8. "Can you analyze my competitors' marketing strategies and provide me with insights?"
9. "I want to know more about my competitors' pricing and sales tactics. Could you do some research on this?"
10. "Could you provide me with a detailed competitive analysis, including my competitors' market share and target audience?"

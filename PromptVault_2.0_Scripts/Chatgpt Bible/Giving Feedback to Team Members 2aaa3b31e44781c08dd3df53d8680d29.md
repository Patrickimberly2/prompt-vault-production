```jsx

```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "How can I provide feedback to my team members in a way that is constructive, clear, and actionable?"
2. "What are some best practices for giving feedback that encourages growth and development, while avoiding demotivating or demoralizing team members?"
3. "Can you recommend any frameworks or models for delivering feedback, such as the 'sandwich' method or the 'SBI' (Situation-Behavior-Impact) model?"
4. "What are some common mistakes to avoid when giving feedback, such as using vague language, making assumptions, or being too critical?"
5. "How can I tailor my feedback to different team members, taking into account their individual strengths, weaknesses, and communication styles?"
6. "What are some common mistakes to avoid when giving feedback to team members, and how can I ensure that I'm providing feedback that is fair, objective, and respectful?"
7. "Can you suggest any feedback training or coaching programs that can help me improve my feedback skills and become a more effective leader?"
8. "What are some ways to encourage team members to seek feedback and take ownership of their own development, rather than relying solely on my input?"
9. "How can I follow up with team members after giving feedback, and track their progress and growth over time?"
10. "What are some ways to foster a culture of continuous feedback and improvement within my team, and encourage team members to give each other feedback and support?"

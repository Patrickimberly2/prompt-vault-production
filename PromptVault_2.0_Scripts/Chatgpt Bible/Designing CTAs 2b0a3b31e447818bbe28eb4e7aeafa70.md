# Designing CTAs

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
Suggest 5 different call-to-actions for this text: **[copy and paste text].**
```

```jsx
Suggest the best call to action for a **[home/product/about]** page for a **[type of business].**
```

```jsx
Suggest 5 ways I can tell people to **[call to action]** in a more persuasive way.
```

### OPEN-ENDED **PROMPTS:**

1. “Can you help me come up with a strong call-to-action for my landing page that encourages visitors to sign up for my newsletter?”
2. “I need a compelling call-to-action for my social media post promoting my new product. Can you help me with that?”
3. “Can you suggest a powerful call-to-action for my email marketing campaign that encourages subscribers to purchase my new course?”
4. “I'm running a Facebook ad campaign for my coaching services. Can you come up with a persuasive call-to-action that encourages people to book a discovery call with me?”
5. “Can you create a strong call-to-action for my website that encourages visitors to download my free ebook?”
6. “I need a clear and effective call-to-action for my YouTube video that encourages viewers to subscribe to my channel.”
7. “Can you help me write a compelling call-to-action for my webinar registration page that encourages people to sign up?”
8. “I'm launching a new podcast and I need a catchy call-to-action for my intro that encourages listeners to tune in for future episodes.”
9. “Can you suggest a persuasive call-to-action for my sales page that encourages visitors to purchase my online course?”
10. “I need a strong call-to-action for my exit pop-up that encourages website visitors to join my email list before leaving. Can you help me with that?”

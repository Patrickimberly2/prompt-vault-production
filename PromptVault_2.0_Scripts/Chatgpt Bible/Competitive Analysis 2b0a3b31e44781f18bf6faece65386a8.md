# Competitive Analysis

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
My top competitors in **[specific industry]** are **[Competitor 1]**, **[Competitor 2],** 
and **[Competitor 3]**. Provide an analysis of their strengths, weaknesses, and 
market positioning.
```

```jsx
I want to identify opportunities for differentiation in **[specific market]**. 
Write a plan to analyze competitor pricing, customer experience, and product 
features.
```

```jsx
Our competitors are known for **[specific strategy or offering]**. Suggest ways 
to counteract this and position our business more effectively.
```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

- What are the key components of a comprehensive competitive analysis?
- How do I identify and benchmark my top competitors effectively?
- What tools can I use to gather insights on competitor strategies and performance?
- How do I assess my competitors’ pricing models and adjust my own accordingly?
- What are the best practices for evaluating competitors’ customer feedback and reviews?
- How can I identify gaps in the market that competitors are not addressing?
- How do I create a SWOT analysis comparing my business to competitors?
- What role does social media play in monitoring competitor activity?
- How do I use competitor data to refine my marketing and sales strategies?
- What are the risks of focusing too much on competitors, and how do I avoid them?

# Writing Tweets & Threads

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
Here is a Tweet I wrote: [**insert Tweet**]. Can you help me write a new one about [**topic**]?
**[For Threads add: Make sure it is 75 tokens or less]**.
```

```jsx
I want to write a **[Twitter/Threads]** thread about [**topic**]. I want to make it engaging and generate leads for my [**niche**] coaching business. Give me the best ways to structure this thread.
```

```jsx
What are good **[Twitter/Threads]** threads a [**niche**] consultant could write to reach more people? 
```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "Can you provide me with some tips on how to create engaging Twitter/Threads posts for my coaching business?"
2. "What are some effective ways to structure Twitter threads that will attract potential clients for my consulting services?"
3. "As a coach, what type of content should I be sharing on Twitter/Threads to build my brand and attract new clients?"
4. "Can you offer any advice on how to use Twitter/Threads to connect with other coaches and consultants in my industry?"
5. "How can I use Twitter/Threads to share my expertise and build my reputation as a thought leader in my field of consulting?"
6. "What are some creative ways to use Twitter/Threads to promote my coaching services and drive traffic to my website?"
7. "What types of visuals or media should I include in my Twitter/Threads posts to make them more engaging for potential clients?"
8. "Can you recommend any tools or apps that can help me schedule and manage my Twitter/Threads content more efficiently as a coach or consultant?"
9. "What are some best practices for using hashtags effectively in my Twitter posts and threads to increase visibility and engagement?"
10. "How can I use Twitter/Threads to build relationships with my coaching clients or consulting partners, and provide value to them outside of our formal engagements?"

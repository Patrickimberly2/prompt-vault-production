# Content Strategy Development

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
My target audience consists of **[specific demographic/interest group]**. Create 
a content strategy on **[topic]** to engage them and achieve **[specific goal].**
```

```jsx
Our competitors publish content like **[specific type, e.g., blog posts, videos].**
You can check out their approach at **[competitor’s website link]**. Suggest a 
unique angle for our content to stand out in [industry].
```

```jsx
Create a 6-month content strategy for **[tech/real estate/marketing]** targeting 
**[tech professionals/first-time homebuyers/small business owners]**. Focus on 
addressing their key challenges, such as **[insert challenges, e.g., adapting 
to new marketing tools, finding affordable properties, boosting brand 
visibility]**. 

Suggest content types like **[reviews/guides/case studies/
strategies]** and recommend the best platforms **[Instagram/LinkedIn/YouTube]** for 
each type. 

Include a posting schedule, content themes, and engagement tactics 
to increase leads, conversions, and brand awareness.
```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

- How do I identify the right content types for my target audience?
- What tools can I use to develop a high-impact content marketing strategy?
- How can I perform a gap analysis to find opportunities in my content?
- What are the best practices for creating a content calendar?
- How do I set measurable goals for content marketing?
- What is the most effective way to repurpose long-form content into bite-sized pieces?

- How can I align my content strategy with my sales funnel?
- What types of content work best for different stages of the buyer’s journey?
- How do I prioritize content ideas when I have limited resources?
- What metrics should I track to measure the success of my content strategy?

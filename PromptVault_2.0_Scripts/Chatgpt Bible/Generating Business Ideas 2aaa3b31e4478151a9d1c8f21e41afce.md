# Generating Business Ideas

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
I want to help **[target customer]** with **[customer needs]** by **[describe services/activities/strategies]**. 

Suggest 5 potential ideas for my new **[type]** business. Add some potential names for each idea.
```

```jsx
What resources do I need for starting my **[type]** business?
****Detail them by:

****- Physical resources
- Financial resources
- Human resources
- Digital resources 
```

```jsx
I want to start a business in the **[industry]**.
Give me a step-by-step guide on how I can identify the most important needs
of the people in the **[industry]** that I can serve with my products or services.
```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "Can you help me brainstorm some potential business ideas?"
2. "I'm looking for inspiration for a new business venture, can you offer some suggestions?"
3. "What are some emerging markets or industries that I should consider for a new business?"
4. "Can you help me identify a gap in the market that I could fill with a new business idea?"
5. "What are some innovative business models that I could explore?"
6. "How can I identify a target audience and develop a business idea that meets their needs?"
7. "Can you provide some examples of successful business ideas that have taken off in recent years?"
8. "What are some tools or techniques I can use to generate new business ideas?"
9. "How can I leverage my skills and expertise to develop a new business idea?"
10. "Can you help me assess the feasibility and potential profitability of a business idea?"

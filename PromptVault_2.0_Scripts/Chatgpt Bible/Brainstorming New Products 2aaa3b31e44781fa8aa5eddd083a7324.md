# Brainstorming New Products

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
My business works in **[industry]** and we want to launch an innovative product that fills these needs:
**[list of needs]**

Give me some original ideas for this new product.
```

```jsx
My brand's core business is **[type of product or service]**.
Looking at customer surveys and market research it turns out people need **[brief description of what they need]**.
How can I create a new product that fills this need?
```

```jsx
I am developing a new **[type of product]** for the **[industry]**. The goal of this product is to **[goal]**.
What features should I include in this **[type of product]**?
```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "Can you help me generate new product ideas?"
2. "What are the latest trends in my industry that I could capitalize on with a new product?"
3. "How can I create products that address my customers' unmet needs?"
4. "What are some untapped niches in my industry that I could develop products for?"
5. "Can you help me evaluate the feasibility of my new product ideas?"
6. "What are the key features and benefits that my new product should offer?"
7. "How can I differentiate my new product from competitors in the market?"
8. "What are the potential risks and challenges in developing a new product?"
9. "What are some innovative product ideas that could disrupt my industry?"
10. "What are the most successful product launches in my industry and what can I learn from them?"

# Brainstorming Your Company Values

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
I am a business owner in the **[industry]**. What questions should I ask myself to find my company values? 
```

```c
Give me a step-by-step guide on how a **[industry]** business owner can find their company values.
```

```jsx
Give me 10 examples of company values that every a healthy workplace in the [**industry**] needs to have.
```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "Can you suggest some strategies for identifying and articulating our company's core values?"
2. "What are some examples of company values that have been effective in building a strong and positive company culture?"
3. "How can we involve our team in the process of developing and refining our company values?"
4. "Do you have any advice for ensuring that our company values are aligned with our mission, vision, and goals?"
5. "What are some ways to communicate our company values effectively to our team and stakeholders?"
6. "How can we ensure that our company values are reflected in our hiring, training, and performance management processes?"
7. "What are some ways to measure and track the impact of our company values on our business outcomes and employee engagement?"
8. "Can you provide some guidance on how to prioritize and balance different values that may be important to our business?"
9. "What are some common mistakes to avoid when developing and communicating company values?"
10. "How can we ensure that our company values are inclusive and reflect the diversity of our team and stakeholders?"

# Generating internal policy documents

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
Please generate a policy document that delineates the guidelines governing the conduct of **[employee/contractor/vendor]** on company **[premises/property]**. This document should include policies pertaining to **[smoking/alcohol consumption/substance abuse/weapons possession]**, as well as regulations regarding access to **[restricted areas/secure facilities]**.
```

```jsx
Please produce a thorough policy document that provides a comprehensive overview of our company's stance on **[data protection/information security]**. This document should encompass guidelines concerning **[data handling/access management/security controls]**, as well as **[data retention/destruction policies/data breach response plan]**.
```

```jsx
Please generate a policy document that details the processes involved in managing and **[approving/reviewing/processing] [expense reports/travel requests/purchase orders]**. This document should include guidelines concerning **[reimbursable expenses/budgetary limits/approval authority]**, as well as the necessary **[documentation/receipts/approval forms]** that are required.
```

6. "What considerations should businesses keep in mind when establishing disciplinary actions and consequences within their internal policy documents?"
7. "What methods can businesses use to gather feedback and input from employees during the creation or revision of internal policy documents?"
8. "How can businesses ensure that their internal policy documents are accessible and inclusive, considering diverse employee backgrounds and needs?"

# Writing Proposals for Clients

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK PROMPTS**:**

```jsx
Write a proposal for my **[potential client]** who is struggling with **[pain points]**. These are the services that I offer **[list services]**. Make the proposal **[describe the tone of voice]**.
```

```jsx
Write a proposal for my potential **[client]** who is struggling with **[pain points]**. These are the services that I offer **[list services]**. Here is my proposed **[pricing]** and **[timeline]** for delivery. Make the proposal **[describe the tone of voice]**.
```

```jsx
I am pitching a project to **[insert company/organization name]** and need to write a proposal. The project is focused on **[insert project scope]**. What are the key deliverables and timeline for the project? How will I ensure that the project meets their needs? Write a proposal that is concise and persuasive.
```

### OPEN-ENDED **PROMPTS:**

1. “How can [services] help a client business in [industry] achieve its goals and objectives?”
2. “How can a business in [industry] stand out from competitors?”
3. “How can we help [type of business] improve the efficiency and productivity of another business?”
4. “What solutions can a [type of business] provide to address any current pain points or challenges in the market?”
5. “How can a [type of business] assist a client in expanding its customer base and reaching new markets?”
6. What steps can a [type of business] take to improve customer satisfaction and loyalty?”
7. “How can our [type of services] stay ahead of industry trends and innovation?”
8. “How can [type of industry] demonstrate the ROI and potential impact of its services to clients?”

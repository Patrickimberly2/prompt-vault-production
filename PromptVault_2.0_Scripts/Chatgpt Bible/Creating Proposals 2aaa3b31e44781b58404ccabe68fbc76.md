# Creating Proposals

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
I have a [**coaching/consulting**] business and I help [**audience**] with [**problem**]. I send proposals after a sales call. How can I structure the proposal in a way that turns leads into clients?
```

```jsx
I help [**audience**] [**outcome**]. How can I package my services in a way that they want to work with me long-term?
```

```jsx
I just had a call with a potential client who is having [**problem**] and wants to [**outcome**]. My way to help [**him/her**] is: [**brief description of your offer, package of services and pricing**].
Write an effective proposal for my client.
```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "Can you provide me with some tips on how to create a winning proposal for my coaching and consulting business?"
2. "What are some best practices for creating effective proposals that resonate with potential clients?"
3. "How can I make my proposals stand out and grab the attention of potential clients in the coaching and consulting industry?"
4. "What are some key elements that I should include in my proposals to highlight my expertise and demonstrate my value to potential clients?"
5. "Do you have any suggestions for structuring my proposals in a way that is easy to follow and understand for potential clients?"
6. "How can I tailor my proposals to the specific needs and goals of each potential client?"
7. "Are there any common mistakes that I should avoid when creating proposals for my coaching and consulting business?"
8. "What are some effective strategies for presenting pricing and payment options in my proposals?"
9. "Can you recommend any tools or resources that can help me streamline the proposal creation process and make it more efficient?"
10. "How can I use storytelling and case studies in my proposals to demonstrate my expertise and build trust with potential clients?"

# Writing Job Descriptions

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
I am a [**niche**] [**coach/consultant**] for [**audience**]. I am looking for a [**role you want to fill**]. I am looking for someone [**brief description of who you are looking for**]. What are the best platforms I can use to attract good candidates?
```

```jsx
Here is a job post I wrote: "[**insert job post here**]"
Write a new job description with the same structure and tone of voice, but this time make it tailored for [**role you want to fill**]. I want someone who is [**brief description of who you are looking for**].
```

```jsx
I am a [**niche**] [**coach/consultant**] for [**audience**]. I am looking for a [**role you want to fill**]. I am looking for someone [**brief description of who you are looking for**]. When they apply for the job they need to [**brief description of the hiring process**]. Write a job post following these instructions.
```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "What are some best practices for writing a job description for a coaching business?"
2. "How can I ensure that my job description accurately reflects the skills and experience needed for the position?"
3. "What are some effective ways to showcase the benefits of working for my coaching business in the job description?"
4. "How can I make my job description stand out from other coaching businesses in the market?"
5. "What are some common mistakes to avoid when writing a job description for a coaching business?"
6. "How can I tailor my job description to attract the right candidates for the position?"
7. "What information should I include in the job description to ensure that candidates have a clear understanding of the position?"
8. "How can I ensure that my job description is inclusive and appeals to a diverse pool of candidates?"
9. "What are some effective ways to communicate the company culture and values in the job description?"
10. "How can I use language and tone to effectively convey the personality and character of my coaching business in the job description?"

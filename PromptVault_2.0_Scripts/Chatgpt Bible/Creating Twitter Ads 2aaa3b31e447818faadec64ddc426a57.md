# Creating Twitter Ads

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
You are an A-list social media manager specialized in growing Twitter accounts. I am a [**course creator/consultant**] for [**target audience**]. Can you give me ways to create a converting Twitter ad?
```

```jsx
You are an A-list media buyer. What's the best format for Twitter ads that convert? I want to get high-quality leads for my [**niche**] coaching business.
```

```jsx
Here is my best performing tweet about [**topic**]: [**insert tweet**]. I want to create an ad on the same topic to attract people to my [**course/program/mastermind**] for [**target audience**]. With that in mind, can you write three Twitter ads?
```

1. "Can you give me some tips on creating effective Twitter ads that will help me reach more people as a coach/consultant?"

3. "What kind of targeting options should I consider when creating Twitter ads as a coach/consultant to ensure they reach the right audience?"
4. "Are there any specific Twitter ad formats that are particularly effective for coaches/consultants? If so, what are they?"
5. "How can I use Twitter ads to generate leads for my coaching/consulting business?"
6. "Can you provide examples of successful Twitter ads for coaches/consultants that I can use for inspiration?"
7. "What kind of messaging should I use in my Twitter ads as a coach/consultant to attract potential clients?"
8. "How can I make my Twitter ads stand out from my competitors as a coach/consultant?"
9. "What kind of budget should I allocate to my Twitter ads as a coach/consultant to ensure they are effective?"
10. "What are some common mistakes coaches/consultants make when creating Twitter ads, and how can I avoid them?"

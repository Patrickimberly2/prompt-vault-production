### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "What are some relaxation techniques that I can use to manage stress, such as deep breathing exercises, mindfulness meditation, or progressive muscle relaxation?"
2. "Can you recommend any stress management training or coaching programs that can help me build resilience and cope with pressure more effectively?"
3. "How can I prioritize my tasks and delegate responsibilities to my team members, in order to reduce my workload and avoid burnout?"
4. "What are some strategies for managing conflict and resolving disputes within my team, such as active listening, reframing, or compromise?"
5. "How can I maintain a positive attitude and outlook during challenging times, and avoid becoming overwhelmed or discouraged?"
6. "What are some ways to manage time more efficiently, and avoid procrastination or distraction?"
7. "How can I set realistic goals and expectations for myself and my team, and avoid overcommitting or underdelivering?"
8. "What are some strategies for staying organized and keeping track of deadlines, such as using a planner or project management software?"
9. "Can you recommend any stress reduction activities that I can incorporate into my daily routine, such as exercise, journaling, or spending time in nature?"
10. "What are some ways to build a supportive network of colleagues, mentors, or friends who can provide guidance, advice, and emotional support during stressful situations?"

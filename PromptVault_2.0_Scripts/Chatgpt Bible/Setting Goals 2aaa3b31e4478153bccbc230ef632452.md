# Setting Goals

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
I am a [**coach/consultant**] for [**audience**]. When I do one to one sessions with them I always get them to set goals for their journey. Give me effective strategies and resources I can use to help them identify and set their goals.
```

```jsx
I run a [**coaching/consulting**] business in the [**niche**]. I want my clients reach their goal, that's why I want them to set a goal that is clear, achievable and measurable. Can you give me ideas and methods I can use with my clients?
```

```jsx
In my coaching business I do many one-to-one coaching sessions with [**target audience**], but many of my clients struggle to stay focused on their goals. What's the best way for a coach to help clients set and achieve their goals, so that they are happier?
```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "What are some effective strategies for helping clients define clear and achievable goals during coaching sessions?"
2. "Can you recommend any goal-setting frameworks or methodologies that I can use to guide my clients towards more effective goal-setting?"
3. "How can I help clients break down larger goals into smaller, more manageable tasks?"
4. "What are some best practices for aligning client goals with their values and priorities?"
5. "What types of questions can I ask to help clients clarify and articulate their goals?"
6. "How can I encourage clients to set specific, measurable, actionable, relevant, and time-bound (SMART) goals during our coaching sessions?"
7. "What are some techniques for helping clients create a clear and compelling vision for their future?"
8. "How can I help clients stay accountable to their goals and make progress towards them over time?"
9. "Are there any resources or tools I can provide to clients to help them stay organized and focused on their goals?"
10. "Can you suggest any exercises or activities that I can use to help clients overcome common obstacles to goal-setting, such as fear, uncertainty, or lack of direction?"

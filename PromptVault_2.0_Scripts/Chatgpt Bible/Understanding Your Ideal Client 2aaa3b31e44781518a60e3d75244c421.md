```jsx
What are the best tools I can use to better understand my ideal client when they buy through **[sales calls, online funnels, etc.]**. 
```

```jsx

```

```jsx
My business sells **[type of product]**, and my ideal client is **[brief description with age, job, etc.]**.

```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "Can you help me create a detailed profile of my ideal client?"
2. "What demographic and psychographic characteristics define my ideal customer?"
3. "How can I identify the pain points and motivations of my ideal client?"
4. "What channels does my ideal client use to search for products/services?"
5. "What are the common objections my ideal client has when considering a purchase?"
6. "How can I craft messaging that resonates with my ideal client?"
7. "What are the key factors that influence my ideal client's buying decision?"
8. "What kind of experience does my ideal client expect when interacting with my brand?"
9. "What are the common misconceptions my ideal client has about my industry?"
10. "How can I differentiate my brand and offerings to attract my ideal client?"

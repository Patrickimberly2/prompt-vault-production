# Generating Ideas for Engaging YouTube videos

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
Can you give me ideas for a YouTube organic video for a [**niche**] coach, maybe on topics like [**insert 3 topics you are an expert on**].
```

```jsx
My business sells courses on how to [**what your course teaches**]. What are some creative ideas for YouTube video content that would attract [**target audience**] and generate good engagement?
```

```jsx
Give me the best ways I can find ideas for my next YouTube video. My target audience is [**audience**] and I am specialized in [**expertise**].
```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "What are some trending topics in my industry that I can create a YouTube video on?"
2. "Can you suggest some unique and creative video ideas for my YouTube channel?"
3. "How can I come up with fresh content ideas for my YouTube videos?"
4. "What are some frequently asked questions in my field that I can answer in a YouTube video?"
5. "Can you help me brainstorm some engaging YouTube video ideas that my audience will love?"
6. "What are some current events or trends that I can incorporate into my YouTube video content?"
7. "How can I use my personal experiences to create compelling YouTube video content?"
8. "What are some common misconceptions in my industry that I can address in a YouTube video?"
9. "What are some tips or tricks that I can share with my audience in a YouTube video?"
10. "How can I create a YouTube video that provides value to my viewers and helps to establish me as an expert in my field?"

# Analysing Your Speech

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
Here is the speech about [**topics**] that I will give at an event with [**type of people attending**]: "[**insert speech**]".
Can you help me improve it?
```

```jsx
I just wrote a speech about [**topics**] and I want to see if it's comprehensive enough.
When talking about [**specific topic of your speech**] what are the most important concepts to cover?
```

```jsx
I recently gave a speech about [**topic**] at an event. I am watching the video of my presentation. What are the things I should look for to find what I can improve?
```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "What are some effective methods for analyzing a speech, and how can I apply them to my own presentations?"
2. "How can I evaluate the structure and flow of my speech to ensure that it is engaging and easy to follow for my audience?"
3. "What are some techniques for assessing the tone and style of my speech, and how can I ensure that it matches the message I want to convey?"
4. "What are some ways to gauge audience feedback and reactions to my speech, and how can I use this information to improve future presentations?"
5. "How can I objectively assess the content of my speech to ensure that it is relevant, accurate, and informative for my audience?"
6. "What are some tools and resources that I can use to help analyze and improve my speeches?"
7. "How can I identify and address any areas of weakness or improvement in my delivery, such as body language, eye contact, or vocal tone?"
8. "What are some strategies for identifying and addressing common mistakes or errors in my speeches, such as rambling, repetition, or excessive use of filler words?"
9. "How can I use data and analytics to analyze the effectiveness of my speeches and identify areas for improvement?"
10. "What are some techniques for reflecting on my own performance and identifying areas where I can improve my speech delivery and content?"

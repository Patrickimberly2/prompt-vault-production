# Identifying Your Unique Brand Voice

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
You are an A-list copywriter and brand voice expert.
My brand sells **[product]** to **[target audience]**.
My brand is **[3 adjectives]**. Can you help me identify my own unique brand voice?
I want to stand out from the competitors and [**goal**].
```

```jsx
My business sells **[product]**.
My values are: **[brief list of your core values]**.
What brand voice should I use to make by branding aligned and credible?
```

```jsx
What questions do I need to ask myself to find the unique brand voice for my **[type of business]** business in the **[niche]**?
```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "How can I ensure that my brand's messaging is consistent and on-brand across all of my marketing channels?"
2. "What steps can I take to develop a clear and distinct brand voice that resonates with my target audience?"
3. "Are there any tools or resources that can help me better understand my target customer and craft messaging that speaks directly to their needs and pain points?"
4. "What are some examples of successful brands with strong, distinctive voices, and how can I apply those principles to my own business?"
5. "How can I differentiate my brand's messaging from that of my competitors, while still staying true to my brand's core values and mission?"
6. "What tone and language should I use to convey my brand's personality and values in my marketing messaging?"
7. "Are there any cultural or societal factors that I should consider when developing my brand's voice and messaging?"
8. "What role do visuals and other design elements play in shaping my brand's overall voice and identity?"
9. "How can I test and refine my brand's messaging over time to ensure that it remains relevant and engaging to my target audience?"
10. "What are some common mistakes that businesses make when developing their brand voice, and how can I avoid them?"

# Brainstorming Titles for Your Book

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
I am a [**coach/consultant**] for [**audience**]. I help them [**outcome**]. I am writing a book about [**topic**]. I want to [**goal**]. Give me 10 powerful and attractive title ideas I can use for my book.
```

```jsx
You are an expert book editor. What is an effective title formula coaches and consultants in the [**niche**] can use in their book to sell many copies?
```

```jsx
I wrote a book about [**topics**]. It's tailored for [**audience**]. What is an attractive book title for this kind of audience?
```

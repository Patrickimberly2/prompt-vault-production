8. "What role do customer testimonials and case studies play in the sales funnel, and how can I use them to build trust and credibility with prospects?"
9. "How can I reduce friction and eliminate barriers to conversion throughout the sales funnel?"
10. "What are some common mistakes that businesses make when designing their sales funnels, and how can I avoid them?"

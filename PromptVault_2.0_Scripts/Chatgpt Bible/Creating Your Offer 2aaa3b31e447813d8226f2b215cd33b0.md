```jsx
I am a [**niche**] coach for [**target audience**]. I am creating a program that [**what the program helps with**]. Give me ideas on how I can structure my offer.
```

```jsx
As a [**niche**] course creator I am creating a course on [**topic**]: what topics and resources should I include in my course so that [**target audience**] want to buy?
```

```jsx
I work as a [**coach/consultant**] in the [**industry**]. My audience's biggest problem at the moment is [**problem your audience is facing**]. How can I structure my offer in a way that I help them solve this problem, and feel better?
```

8. "What role does pricing play in creating a winning offer, and how can I determine the right price for my services?"

# Identifying Your Unique Value Proposition

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
My **[industry]** business serves **[target audience]**, who **[pain points]**. We have a **[product/service]** that **[describe the unique benefits]**.

Help me create a clear and concise Unique Value Proposition (UVP) that communicates my unique value to potential customers or clients.
```

```jsx
Pretend you are an A-list branding expert. My business helps **[target audience] [outcome]** by **[unique solution]**. Give me 5 alternatives of effective unique value proposition I can use.
```

```jsx
I run a business in the **[industry]**. We help **[target audience] [outcome]**.
The main benefits are **[list 3 benefit your customers experience]**.
We are unique because **[list the reasons why your business is unique]**.
Write me an effective Unique Value Proposition I can use in my business.
```

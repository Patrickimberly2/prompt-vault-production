# Creating a Title for Your Speech

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
I am writing a speech about [**main topics of the speech**]. More specifically I will cover [**3-5 main concepts you will cover**]. Can you give me some title ideas for my speech? I want people to feel curious and engaged.
```

```jsx
I wrote a speech about [**topic**] with the title "[**insert title**]". Give me 5 title alternatives that keep the same angle.
```

```jsx
In my next speech I will talk about [**brief description of what you are going to cover**]. What is the best angle I can use in the title of the speech to make listeners engaged?
```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "What are some strategies for creating an effective title for my speech?"
2. "Can you provide some tips for crafting a title that will capture the attention of my audience?"
3. "How can I ensure that my speech title accurately conveys the content and purpose of my presentation?"
4. "What are some common mistakes to avoid when creating a title for a speech?"
5. "What are some creative techniques for brainstorming and developing an engaging title for my speech?"
6. "How can I use language and phrasing to make my speech title stand out and pique the interest of my audience?"
7. "What are some examples of effective speech titles and what can I learn from them?"
8. "How can I tailor my speech title to my specific audience and their interests or needs?"
9. "What role does humor or wit play in creating a memorable and effective title for a speech?"
10. "What are some resources or tools that I can use to help me create an impactful and compelling title for my speech?"

# Identifying Your Unique Leadership Style (1)

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
Provide me with the top 3 most popular leadership style assessments used in **[industry]** industry. 

Based on the results of the assessment, how can I define and make use of my own leadership style?
```

```jsx
I am the owner/manager of a **[type]** business,  and I have been operating in the market for **[number of months/years]**. Our company's vision is to **[describe vision]**, and our main 
business goal is to **[goal]**. As the leader of the company, my approach is guided by these core values **[value 1, value 2, value 3]**. 

Write a description of my unique leadership style and recommend the skills I can best leverage to excel as a leader.
```

```jsx
Following recent feedback on my performance as a  **[manager/CEO/team leader]**, there were identified areas for improvement. These include  **[explain areas of improvement/challenges to overcome]**. 

List strategies that can help me address them and become an excellent leader.
```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "Can you help me identify my natural leadership strengths and how I can leverage them to become a more effective leader?"
2. "What are some common leadership styles, and which one(s) might be most suitable for my personality, values, and goals?"
3. "What are some strategies for developing my leadership skills and adapting my style to different situations and team dynamics?"
4. "Can you recommend any personality or leadership assessments that I can take to gain a better understanding of my strengths, weaknesses, and leadership style?"
5. "How can I use feedback from my team, colleagues, and mentors to improve my leadership skills and enhance my unique style?"
6. "What are some ways to establish trust and respect with my team and demonstrate my leadership qualities, such as by setting clear expectations, providing regular feedback, or leading by example?"
7. "Can you suggest any leadership models or frameworks that I can use to guide my decision-making and actions as a leader?"
8. "How can I leverage my unique perspective, experiences, and skills to inspire and motivate my team, and foster a culture of innovation and collaboration?"
9. "What are some best practices for delegating tasks, empowering team members, and promoting a sense of ownership and accountability among my team?"
10. "Can you help me identify any blind spots or areas where I might need to improve my leadership skills, and suggest some strategies for addressing these challenges?"

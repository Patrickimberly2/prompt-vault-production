# Writing an About Me Page

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
You are an A-list copywriter specialized in writing website copy. I am a [**niche**] coach and I help [**target audience**] [**outcome**] through my [**name of the method**].
My story is [**brief summary of your story that is relevant to your audience**]. Can you write me the about page copy?
```

```jsx
I am a [**niche**] coach and I teach [**audience**] how to [**outcome**]. How do I find the most relevelant parts of my personal story that can create a deep relationship with the reader?
```

```jsx
I am a [**niche**] coach, but before this I was [**beginning of your journey**]: everything changed when [**key moment**]. Write an about page for my website based on this. Make it emotional, inspiring and optimistic.
```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "What are some key elements to include in an effective about page for a coach and consultant?"
2. "Can you provide examples of successful about page copy for coaches and consultants?"
3. "What should be the tone and style of the about page to connect with potential clients?"
4. "How do I make my about page stand out and differentiate myself from competitors?"
5. "What is the ideal length for an about page and how do I keep it engaging?"
6. "What should be the primary focus of the about page - personal background, qualifications, or services offered?"
7. "How do I showcase my unique selling proposition and value proposition on my about page?"
8. "Are there any common mistakes to avoid when writing an about page for a coach or consultant?"
9. "What should I include in the call-to-action on my about page?"
10. "How can I ensure that my about page accurately reflects my brand and mission as a coach and consultant?"

# Writing a Contact Me Page

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
You are an A-list copywriter specialized in writing website copy. I am a [**niche**] coach and I help [**target audience**] [**outcome**].
I want people to contact me through [**insert medium**].  Can you write me the about page copy?
```

```jsx
I am a [**niche**] coach and I teach [**audience**] how to [**outcome**]. What's the best way to bring people to contact me through [**medium you use**]?
```

```jsx
I am a [**niche**] coach and I help [**target audience**] [**outcome**]. Give me the best medium I can use to let prospective clients contact me. I want them to feel [**adjective**].
```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "What are the essential elements to include in a 'Contact Me' page copy for a coach or consultant?"
2. "Can you provide me with some examples of effective 'Contact Me' page copy for coaches and consultants?"
3. "What should be the tone and style of the 'Contact Me' page to encourage visitors to contact the coach or consultant?"
4. "How can I make my 'Contact Me' page stand out and entice visitors to take action?"
5. "What is the ideal length for a 'Contact Me' page copy and how do I keep it engaging?"
6. "What should I include in the contact form on my 'Contact Me' page?"
7. "How do I convey my unique selling proposition and value proposition in my 'Contact Me' page copy?"
8. "Are there any common mistakes to avoid when writing a 'Contact Me' page copy for a coach or consultant?"
9. "What should be the primary focus of the 'Contact Me' page - personal background, qualifications, or services offered?"
10. "How can I ensure that my 'Contact Me' page accurately reflects my brand and mission as a coach or consultant?"

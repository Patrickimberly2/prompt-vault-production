# Building Your Speaking Career

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
I am a [**coach/consultant**] for [**audience**]. I want to build my public speaking career. Can you give me ways to do it faster?
```

```jsx
I want to build a speaking career because I want to [**goal**]. Give me 10 strategies to do it.
```

```jsx
In the past I gave some speeches about [**topics**] to [**audience**]. How can I leverage those speeches to get invited to more events?
```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "What are some strategies for building a successful speaking career, and how can I apply them to my own goals?"
2. "What are some key qualities or skills that successful speakers possess, and how can I develop them?"
3. "What are some ways to identify and target my ideal audience, and how can I tailor my message to resonate with them?"
4. "How can I build my reputation as a speaker and establish myself as an authority in my field?"
5. "What are some ways to network with other speakers and industry professionals, and how can these connections benefit my career?"
6. "What are some common challenges that speakers face in building their careers, and how can I overcome them?"
7. "What are some effective marketing and promotion strategies for building my brand as a speaker?"
8. "How can I improve my presentation skills and overcome stage fright or anxiety when speaking in public?"
9. "What are some ways to monetize my speaking career, such as through sponsorships, book deals, or consulting opportunities?"
10. "What are some resources or training programs that can help me develop my speaking career, such as mentorship programs, workshops, or online courses?"

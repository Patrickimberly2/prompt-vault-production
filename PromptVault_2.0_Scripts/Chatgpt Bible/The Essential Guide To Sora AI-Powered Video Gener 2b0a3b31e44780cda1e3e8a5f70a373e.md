# The Essential Guide To Sora: AI-Powered Video Generation

This guide will show you how to use Sora, OpenAI’s new powerful video generator, to create professional, high-quality videos— *without any actors or studio equipment and at almost no cost.*

**THIS GUIDE INCLUDES:**

✅ **90+ ready-made prompts** you can use to generate social media videos for Tiktok, Instagram and Youtube **in seconds.**

✅ Step-by-step instructions on how to create AI videos that match your unique brand, style and content needs 

✅ `How-to’s for making eye-catching video ads for any industry **WITHOUT ever recording a single second of footage yourself**

**HOW TO GET STARTED**

1. Begin by checking out the [**How to Use It](How%20to%20Use%20It%202b0a3b31e447811dbc7fcaaf7c3cc19f.md), [Guidelines](Guidelines%202b0a3b31e44781c5a2ace04b8c308b2d.md)**, and [**Keywords](Keywords%202b0a3b31e44781ed8882d320b1b35d0e.md)** sections to learn how to craft effective video prompts.

1. Choose from a wide range of categories, including: Tiktok, Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, LinkedIn, Technology and Gadget, Health and Fitness, Fashion and Beauty, or Food and Beverage.

*You can either follow the suggestions to create your own prompts tailored to your needs or simply copy and paste from the ready-made prompts provided for each category.*

# Creating a Launch Plan

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
As a [**niche**] [**coach/consultant**] I am launching a book about [**topics**]. My goal is [**goal**]. Can you give me ideas I can use to create my launch plan?
```

```jsx
I am a coach in the [**industry**]. What strategies can I use to create hype about the lauch of my new book? It's about [**topics**].
```

```jsx
My new book is called [**title of the book**] and will be released on [**date of the release**]. The target audience is [**audience**]. Give me an effective launch plan that allows me to generate high-quality leads from this book.
```

6. "How can I use email marketing to promote my book launch as a coach and consultant?"
7. "Are there any best practices for scheduling a book launch event as a coach and consultant?"
8. "What are some effective ways to leverage media outlets to promote my book launch as a coach and consultant?"

10. "What are some key performance indicators (KPIs) to measure the success of my book launch as a coach and consultant?"

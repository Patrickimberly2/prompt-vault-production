### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "What are some effective strategies for delegating tasks within my business?"
2. "How can I determine which tasks are best suited for delegation?"
3. "What qualities should I look for in an individual I am considering delegating tasks to?"
4. "How can I effectively communicate tasks that I am delegating to others?"
5. "What are some common mistakes to avoid when delegating tasks?"
6. "How can I provide support and guidance to those I am delegating tasks to?"
7. "What are some effective ways to measure the success of delegated tasks?"
8. "How can I ensure that those I am delegating tasks to have the resources and tools they need to complete the task successfully?"
9. "What are some ways to foster trust and build strong relationships with those I am delegating tasks to?"
10. "How can I create a culture of accountability and ownership among those I am delegating tasks to?"

# Writing Captions

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
Here is an Instagram caption I wrote: [**insert caption**]. Can you help me write a new one about [**topic**]?
```

```jsx
I want to write an Instagram caption that sends people to my link in bio. That link is the landing page of my new course for [**target audience**] that want to [**goal**]. Tell me the best ways to write this Instagram caption. Make it engaging and not salesy.
```

```jsx
I am about to launch a new course on how [**topic**]. What could I write in the caption of my next Instagram post to create hype and curiosity?
```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "What are the best practices for writing engaging Instagram captions?"
2. "How can I make my Instagram captions stand out from the crowd?"
3. "What are some tips for writing captions that drive engagement?"
4. "Can you give me some examples of creative Instagram captions?"
5. "What's the best way to structure an Instagram caption for maximum impact?"
6. "How can I use emojis effectively in my Instagram captions?"
7. "What are some common mistakes to avoid when writing Instagram captions?"
8. "How can I tell a compelling story through my Instagram captions?"
9. "What's the ideal length for an Instagram caption?"
10. "How can I use calls to action (CTAs) in my Instagram captions to drive engagement and conversions?"

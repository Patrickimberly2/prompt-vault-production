# Creating lease renewal letters

```jsx
I require your help in drafting a lease renewal letter for **[tenant name]**, who is currently residing at **[rental property adress]**. The existing lease is due to expire on **[lease expiration date]**. Please incorporate details regarding any modifications to **[rental price/lease terms]**, such as **[specific details about rules, regulations, or renewal options]**.
```

Given the impending lease expiration of **[tenant name]** on **[lease expiration date]**, could you guide me in penning an engaging lease renewal letter highlighting their rental history, articulating **[reasons for renewing, such as improvements to the property, new management, or other added benefits]**, and detailing any changes to the lease agreement like **[specific details about changes to lease terms or rental price]**?

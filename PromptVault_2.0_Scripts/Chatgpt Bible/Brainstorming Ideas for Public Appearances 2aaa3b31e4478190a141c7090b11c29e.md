# Brainstorming Ideas for Public Appearances

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
I own a business that **[brief description of what your business does]**. I will appear in **[brief description of the context of the public appearence]**.
What are good ideas I could implement to **[goal]**?
```

```jsx
I am about to go to a networking event where I will hang out with **[type of people]** and talk about **[topics]**. Can you give me some tips to grow my network effectively?
```

```jsx
I will participate in a **[type of event]** where I don't know anyone.
My goal is to **[goal]**. What are some good strategies to start conversations and meet new people?
```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "Can you suggest some unique and attention-grabbing topics for a TED talk or keynote speech?"
2. "What are some ideas for hosting or participating in a panel discussion related to my business or industry?"
3. "How can I effectively use public appearances to establish myself as an expert in my field?"
4. "Can you provide guidance on how to create a memorable and engaging presentation that resonates with the audience?"
5. "What are some tips for creating a dynamic and interactive workshop or training session that attendees will find valuable?"
6. "How can I tailor my public appearance to the interests and needs of the specific audience I'll be addressing?"
7. "What should I do to prepare for a media interview or press conference, and how can I make the most of the opportunity?"
8. "Can you suggest some creative ways to use public appearances to promote my brand or products?"
9. "What are some effective methods for networking and making new connections at public events and appearances?"
10. "How can I measure the impact of my public appearances and use the insights to improve future presentations?"

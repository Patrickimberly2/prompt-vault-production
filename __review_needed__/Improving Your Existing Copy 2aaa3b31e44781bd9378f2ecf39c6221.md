# Improving Your Existing Copy

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
I am a [**niche**] coach and I am selling a [**type of product**] that helps people [**outcome**].
Here is a piece of copy I wrote: [**insert copy**].
Make it more [**adjective**].
```

```jsx
You are an A-list copywriter specialized in coaches and consultants in the [**niche**]. Give me a checklist of what to look for to find the things that need improvement in my [**type of copy**] copy.
```

```jsx
I just wrote the [**type of copy**] copy I wrote for [**product**] about [**topic**]: "[**insert copy**]".
Can you help me optimize the copy so that it's more persuasive and converting?
```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "Can you give me some feedback on this copy I wrote?"
2. "I'm looking for ways to improve my writing. Do you have any suggestions?"
3. "I'm struggling to make this copy more engaging. Any tips?"
4. "I want to make sure my message is clear in this copy. Can you help me with that?"
5. "Do you have any advice for making this copy more persuasive?"
6. "I'm not sure if this copy is hitting the right tone. Can you give me your opinion?"
7. "I want to make sure this copy is easy to read. What changes would you recommend?"
8. "I'm looking for ways to make this copy stand out. Any ideas?"
9. "Is there anything in this copy that's confusing or unclear?"
10. "What do you think are the strengths and weaknesses of this copy, and how can I improve it?"

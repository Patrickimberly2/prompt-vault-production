# Developing a List of FAQs for Customers

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
I wrote a landing page for a **[product or service]**.
**[examplain the main features, benefits and unique elements of the product/service]**
Can you help me come up with a comprehensive list of FAQs to put in the landing page?
```

```jsx
Here is the list of FAQs I came up with for my **[product or service]:
[list of FAQs]**

Give more questions I can add to the list?
```

```jsx
My business **[brief description of what your business offers]**. When prospects read my marketing material they have some doubts:
"**[list of doubts and objections of your prospects]**"

Write a list of FAQs I can put on my website.
```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "How can I create a comprehensive list of FAQs for my business's website or product documentation?"
2. "What are some best practices for developing a useful and informative FAQs section?"
3. "Can you provide guidance on how to structure and organize FAQs effectively?"
4. "Would it be possible for you to review my existing FAQs and suggest improvements?"
5. "How can I anticipate and address common questions that customers may have about my product or service?"
6. "What are the benefits of having a strong FAQs section for my business, and how can I optimize it?"
7. "Can you provide examples of effective FAQs sections on other businesses' websites?"
8. "How can I ensure that my FAQs are clear and easy to understand for customers?"
9. "What tools or resources are available to help me create and manage a FAQs section for my business?"
10. "What should I do to keep my FAQs up-to-date and relevant over time?"

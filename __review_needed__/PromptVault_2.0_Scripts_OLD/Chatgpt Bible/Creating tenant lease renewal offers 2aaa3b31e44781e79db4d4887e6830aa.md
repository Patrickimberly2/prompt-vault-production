# Creating tenant lease renewal offers

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
I need assistance in crafting a lease renewal offer that encompasses a **[percentage]**% rent increase, a **[term length]**-month lease extension, and an enticing **[additional benefit]**. Could you generate a personalized proposal for **[tenant name]**, who has been a valued tenant for **[years]**? The objective is to present a tailored lease renewal offer that acknowledges their tenure and provides attractive terms and benefits to encourage them to continue their tenancy.
```

```jsx
Could you help me create a lease renewal proposal for **[tenant name]**, who has lived here for **[years]**, incorporating **[new term 1]**, **[new term 2]**, and **[new term 3]**? Suggest a **[benefit]** to encourage them to renew their lease.
```

```jsx
Assist me in creating a lease renewal offer tailored to **[tenant name]**'s specific circumstances. Include a **[benefit]** for renewing the lease, and emphasize the unique features of the property, including **[property feature 1]**, **[property feature 2]**, and the attractive aspects of the neighborhood.
```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "What are the specific terms and conditions of the lease renewal offer for [TENANT NAME]?"
2. "How can we customize the lease renewal offer to address [TENANT NAME]'s individual needs and preferences?"
3. "What incentives or benefits can be included in the lease renewal offer to encourage [TENANT NAME] to stay?"
4. "Are there any new terms or provisions that should be incorporated into the lease renewal offer?"
5. "How can we highlight the unique features of the property to make the lease renewal offer more enticing for [TENANT NAME]?"
6. "Are there any special promotions or discounts that can be offered to [TENANT NAME] as part of the lease renewal offer?"
7. "What steps can be taken to showcase the value and convenience of the neighborhood in the lease renewal offer?"
8. "Can we provide any additional services or amenities as a part of the lease renewal offer to enhance [TENANT NAME]'s living experience?"
9. "How can we demonstrate appreciation and recognition for [TENANT NAME]'s loyalty and tenure in the lease renewal offer?"
10. "Are there any opportunities for negotiation or flexibility in the lease renewal offer to accommodate [TENANT NAME]'s specific circumstances?"

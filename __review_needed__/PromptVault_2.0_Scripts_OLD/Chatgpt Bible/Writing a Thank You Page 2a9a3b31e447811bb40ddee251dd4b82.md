# Writing a Thank You Page

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK PROMPTS**:**

```jsx
Write a persuasive **[word count]** landing page offering a **[product]** named **[product name]** that helps **[audience] [benefit] [offer]**. Start by saying "Thank you, your free resource is on the way."
```

### OPEN-ENDED PROMPTS**:**

1. “Can you write a [fill in the blank] page for my new product launch? We want to thank our customers for their support and encourage them to share their experience with others.”
2. “I need a [fill in the blank] page for my nonprofit organization. Can you write copy that expresses our gratitude for donations and encourages people to continue supporting our cause?”
3. “Can you write a [fill in the blank] page for my e-commerce website? We want to thank our customers for their purchase and offer them a special discount on their next order.”
4. “I'm hosting a [fill in the blank] event and need a thank you page for attendees. Can you write copy that expresses our appreciation for their participation and provides information about upcoming events?”
5. “Can you write a [fill in the blank] page for my online course? We want to thank students for enrolling and offer them additional resources to support their learning.”
6. “I need a [fill in the blank] page for my coaching services. Can you write copy that thanks clients for their commitment to personal growth and provides information about future sessions?”
7. “Can you write a [fill in the blank] page for my business conference? We want to thank attendees for their participation and encourage them to continue networking with fellow professionals.”
8. “I'm launching a new product line and need a [fill in the blank] page for customers. Can you write copy that thanks them for trying our products and provides information about new releases?”
9. “Can you write a [fill in the blank] page for my fitness studio? We want to thank our members for their dedication to their health and wellness journey and offer them a special promotion.”
10. “I need a [fill in the blank] page for my restaurant. Can you write copy that thanks customers for dining with us and encourages them to leave a review or follow us on social media?”

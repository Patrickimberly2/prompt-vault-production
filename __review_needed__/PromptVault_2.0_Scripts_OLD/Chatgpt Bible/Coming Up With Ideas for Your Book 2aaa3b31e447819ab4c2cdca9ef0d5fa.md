# Coming Up With Ideas for Your Book

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
I am a [**coach/consultant**] for [**audience**]. I help them [**outcome**] and I want to write a book with that goal in mind. What are some new ideas I can write about in my book?
```

```jsx
You are funnel specialist for coaches and consultants in the [**niche**]. I am a [**coach/consultant**] for [**audience**] and I want to write a book to create authority. Give me 10 powerful ideas I can develop in my book.
```

```jsx
I run a [**coaching/consulting**] business where I help [**target audience**] [**outcome**]. What books can I look at to get ideas for my book?
```

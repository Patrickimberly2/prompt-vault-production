### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "How can you define lead scoring criteria that effectively aligns with your ideal customer profile and identifies the most qualified leads for your sales team?"
2. "What strategies can you employ to assign numerical values or weights to different lead attributes or behaviors to create a comprehensive lead scoring system?"
3. "How do you determine the threshold or benchmark scores that differentiate between a marketing qualified lead (MQL) and a sales qualified lead (SQL)?"
4. "What techniques can you use to prioritize certain lead activities or engagement levels based on their significance and relevance to your sales process?"
5. "How can you incorporate demographic data, such as industry or company size, into your lead scoring criteria to better understand the potential fit of a lead?"
6. "What methods can you employ to track and measure lead interactions across various touchpoints, such as website visits, email opens, or content downloads, to inform your lead scoring?"
7. "How do you assess the quality and accuracy of lead data to ensure the integrity of your lead scoring process?"
8. "What strategies can you use to collaborate with your sales team to gather feedback and insights on lead scoring criteria, ensuring it reflects their needs and requirements?"
9. "How can you regularly review and refine your lead scoring criteria based on the performance and outcomes of previous leads, optimizing your lead qualification process?"
10. "What techniques can you employ to integrate your lead scoring system with your customer relationship management (CRM) software for seamless lead management and tracking?"

# Brainstorming Business Ideas

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
I'm starting a business in the **[describe business]** industry.
Suggest 5 successful business ideas that are currently in high demand in this industry.
```

```jsx
My main strengths are **[strength 1],[strength 2], and [strength 3].** Present the top 5 of business types that I could create.
```

```jsx
I'm looking for business ideas that fulfill **[customer need]**.  It will be designed for **[target audience]**. Generate 10 ideas you have for me.
```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "Can you help me brainstorm some potential business ideas in [industry]?"
2. "I'm looking for some creative business ideas. Could you provide me with some inspiration?"
3. "Can we brainstorm together to come up with some unique business ideas?"
4. "What are some emerging business trends that I should consider when brainstorming ideas?"
5. "Could you provide me with some prompts or exercises to stimulate my thinking when brainstorming business ideas?"
6. "How can I identify unmet needs or gaps in the market when brainstorming business ideas?"
7. "What are some successful business models that I could adapt to create my own business idea?"
8. "How can I leverage my skills and expertise to develop a business idea that aligns with my passions and interests?"
9. "What are some low-cost business ideas that I could consider starting on a tight budget?"
10. "Can you help me evaluate and refine my business ideas to ensure their feasibility and potential for success?"

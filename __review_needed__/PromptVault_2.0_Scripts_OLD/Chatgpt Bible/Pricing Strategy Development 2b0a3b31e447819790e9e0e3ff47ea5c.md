# Pricing Strategy Development

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
My product/service is priced at **[current price]**. Suggest a pricing strategy 
that maximizes revenue and appeals to **[specific target audience]**.
```

```jsx
We are considering introducing tiered pricing. Provide ideas for creating 
**[three/four]** pricing tiers that deliver value to customers at each level.
```

```jsx
Our competitor charges **[competitor’s price]** for a similar product. Suggest a 
competitive pricing strategy that differentiates us while remaining attractive
to consumers.
```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

- What are the most common pricing strategies used in [specific industry]?
- How do I decide whether to use value-based or cost-plus pricing?
- What role does market research play in determining pricing strategy?
- How can I adjust my pricing strategy to account for different customer segments?
- What are the advantages of using psychological pricing techniques (e.g., $9.99 instead of $10)?
- How do I incorporate discounts into my pricing strategy without undermining my brand’s value?
- What are the potential risks of a penetration pricing strategy for a new product?
- How can I use pricing as a tool for customer retention or acquisition?
- How do I assess whether my current pricing is too high or too low for my target market?
- What tools can I use to test different pricing strategies and optimize for maximum profitability?

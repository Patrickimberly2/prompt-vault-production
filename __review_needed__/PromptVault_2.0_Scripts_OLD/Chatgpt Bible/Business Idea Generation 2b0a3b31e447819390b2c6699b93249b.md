# Business Idea Generation

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
I want to brainstorm business ideas in **[specific industry or niche]**. Suggest 
creative methods to identify unmet customer needs and market opportunities.
```

```jsx
My target audience is **[specific demographic]**. Provide a process to generate 
business ideas that solve their key pain points.
```

```jsx
I have skills in **[specific area].** Write a framework to brainstorm business 
ideas that leverage these strengths and interests.
```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

- What are the best techniques for generating unique business ideas?
- How do I identify gaps in the market to inspire innovative business concepts?
- What tools or frameworks can I use to structure a business idea brainstorming session?
- How do I assess the feasibility and potential of new business ideas?
- What role does customer feedback play in refining initial business concepts?
- How can I use trends and data to spark new business ideas?
- What are the best practices for brainstorming business ideas as a team?
- How do I stay creative and open-minded during the ideation process?
- What are some ways to validate business ideas before investing resources?
- How can I turn personal passions into viable business opportunities?

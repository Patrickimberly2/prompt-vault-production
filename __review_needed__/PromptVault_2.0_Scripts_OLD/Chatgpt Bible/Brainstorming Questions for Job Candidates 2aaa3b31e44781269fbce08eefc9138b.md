# Brainstorming Questions for Job Candidates

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
What are the best questions I can ask **[position you want to fill**] to see if we're a good fit?
```

```jsx
You are a HR specialist. I will interview candidates for the **[job title]** position.
The role will involve **[brief description of the job]**. Give me 10 questions I can ask candidates to make sure they are the right choice?
```

```jsx
I am about to interview the candidates for the [**role you want to fill**] for my **[industry]** business.
For this position I am looking for someone who [**brief description**].
What are the some effective questions I can ask candidates?
```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "Can you help me come up with some interview questions that will help me find the right job candidate?"
2. "I'm looking for some questions to ask job candidates during interviews. Do you have any suggestions?"
3. "What are some good questions I can ask during job interviews to really get to know the candidate?"
4. "I'm trying to put together a list of thought-provoking questions to ask job candidates. Can you give me some ideas?"
5. "What are some behavioral interview questions that will help me evaluate job candidates?"
6. "I'm looking for some questions that will help me assess a candidate's skills and experience. What do you recommend?"
7. "Do you have any tips for creating interview questions that will help me identify the best job candidate?"
8. "I'm struggling to come up with interview questions that will help me make a decision. Can you give me some advice?"
9. "What are some non-traditional questions I can ask during job interviews that will help me learn more about the candidate?"
10. "Can you provide me with some interview questions that will help me determine a candidate's potential for growth and development?"

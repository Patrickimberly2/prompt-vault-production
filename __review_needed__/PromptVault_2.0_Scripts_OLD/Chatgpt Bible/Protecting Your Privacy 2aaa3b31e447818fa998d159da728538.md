### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "What are the most important steps I can take to safeguard my personal information and protect my privacy online?"
2. "Can you provide me with some tips or best practices for keeping my online activity and personal data private?"
3. "How can I ensure that my social media accounts and other online profiles are secure and protected from potential cyber threats?"
4. "What are some of the biggest risks to my online privacy, and how can I mitigate them?"
5. "What are some tools or technologies that can help me protect my privacy online, such as VPNs or anti-malware software?"
6. "How can I ensure that my personal data is not being sold or shared with third-party companies without my consent?"
7. "What should I do if I suspect that my personal information has been compromised, such as in a data breach or hack?"
8. "How can I protect my privacy while using public Wi-Fi networks or other shared Internet connections?"
9. "What are some best practices for creating strong and secure passwords, and how often should I change them?"
10. "Can you recommend any resources or guides that can help me stay up-to-date on the latest privacy threats and best practices?"

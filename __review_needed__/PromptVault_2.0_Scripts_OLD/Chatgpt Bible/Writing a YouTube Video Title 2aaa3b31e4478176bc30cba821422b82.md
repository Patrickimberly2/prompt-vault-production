# Writing a YouTube Video Title

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
Write a YouTube video title about [**topic of the video**] that makes people click on the video to watch it.
```

```jsx
Here are some of my YouTube video titles: [**title 1, title 2. title 3**]. Write a new one about [**topic of the video**] that is aligned with the other titles I gave you. Keep in mind YouTube search engine optimization.
```

```jsx
Give me a step-by-step guide on how to write an effective YouTube video title for [**niche**] coaches.
```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "What are some effective strategies for creating a catchy and informative YouTube video title?"
2. "How can I optimize my YouTube video title for search engine visibility?"
3. "What are some best practices for crafting a YouTube video title that accurately reflects the content of the video?"
4. "Can you provide some tips for writing a YouTube video title that captures the attention of my target audience?"
5. "How can I use keywords in my YouTube video title to attract viewers who are interested in my coaching/consulting niche?"
6. "What are some examples of successful YouTube video titles from other coaches/consultants in my industry?"
7. "What are some common mistakes to avoid when writing a YouTube video title?"
8. "How can I make my YouTube video title stand out from other videos on similar topics?"
9. "Can you suggest some creative or unique phrases to use in my YouTube video title that will make it more appealing to viewers?"
10. "What are some ways to test and optimize my YouTube video title to maximize views and engagement?"

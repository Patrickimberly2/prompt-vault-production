# Generating support articles

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
We have been receiving numerous inquiries regarding **[specific issue]**. Would it be possible for you to generate a comprehensive support article that addresses **[related topic]** and offers detailed, step-by-step instructions on how to **[specific action]?**
```

```jsx
As we prepare to launch our new product, we are in need of creating support articles that encompass **[specific features].** Could you generate articles that cover **[feature A], [feature B]**, and **[feature C]**? It would be appreciated if you could incorporate **[specific information]** and include any **[tips or tricks]** that would be beneficial for our customers.
```

```jsx
In order to align our support articles for **[specific product or service]** with recent updates, we require articles that offer up-to-date information on **[topic A], [topic B]**, and **[topic C]**. It would be appreciated if you could incorporate any **[warnings or precautions]** that our customers need to be mindful of.
```

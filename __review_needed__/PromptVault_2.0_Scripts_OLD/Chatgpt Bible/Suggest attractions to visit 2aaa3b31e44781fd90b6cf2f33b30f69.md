# Suggest attractions to visit

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
For a traveler with a **[travel style]** approach, who has a fondness for **[interests]**, and a budget of **[budget]**, what are some of the must-see attractions in **[city]**?
```

```jsx
For individuals seeking suggestions on attractions to visit, what landmarks, sights, or hidden gems do you recommend in **[destination/city/region]**? Explore **[historical sites/natural wonders/cultural hotspots]** that can provide unique experiences.
```

```jsx
Can you recommend **[number]** captivating attractions in **[city/region]** that cater to the preferences of a **[travel style]** traveler, who enjoys [activity] and has a keen interest in **[interests]**?
```

6. "What are some adrenaline-pumping adventure activities or outdoor attractions for thrill-seeking travelers?"
7. "Are there any renowned festivals or events that are worth experiencing during specific times of the year?"
8. "Can you recommend cultural performances, such as traditional dances or music shows, that showcase the local artistic talent?"
9. "What are some unique culinary experiences or food markets that allow travelers to indulge in the local cuisine?"

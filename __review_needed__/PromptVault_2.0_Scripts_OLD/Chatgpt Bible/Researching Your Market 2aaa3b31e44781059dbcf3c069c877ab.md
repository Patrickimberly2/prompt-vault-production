# Researching Your Market

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
I want to send a survey to my best clients to do some research and find how I can come up with ways to improve my **[type of product]**.
Give me 10 questions I can ask my best clients with that goal in mind.
```

```jsx
What things should I look at in my competitors' activity to research the **[niche]** market effectively and find new ideas to implement in my own **[type of product]**?
```

```jsx
I want to improve my **[type of product]**.
What type of content should I analyse to find more about the **[niche]** market wants and needs?
```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "Would you conduct market research for my business?"
2. "Could you provide insights on my target market?"
3. "I'm interested in data on my industry, could you help me gather that?"
4. "What are the trends and competition like in my market?"
5. "Can you analyze consumer behavior in my industry to guide my decisions?"
6. "I need to conduct a SWOT analysis for my business, could you assist me?"
7. "What is the current state of my market and how can I capitalize on it?"
8. "Who are my competitors and what strategies are they using?"
9. "Are there any untapped market opportunities or potential customers?"
10. "How can I determine the viability of my business idea through market research?"

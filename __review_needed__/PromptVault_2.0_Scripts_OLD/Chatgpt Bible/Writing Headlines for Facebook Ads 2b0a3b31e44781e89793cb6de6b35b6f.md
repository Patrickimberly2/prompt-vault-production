# Writing Headlines for Facebook Ads

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK PROMPTS**:**

```jsx
Write 3 Facebook ads with maximum 40 characters based on this ad copy:
**[Copy and paste landing page or ad copy].**
```

```jsx
Provide me 3 examples of headlines that grab attention for **[product type].**
```

```jsx
What are some examples of headlines that effectively convey the main idea of **[topic]**?
```

```jsx
Give me examples of catchy headlines for a **[topic or product]** that will make people want to **[desired action]**. Make them BuzzFeed style.
```

### OPEN-ENDED PROMPTS**:**

1. “Can you generate attention-grabbing headlines for my Facebook ads promoting my new [fill in the product/service] that [fill in the benefit]?”
2. “I need catchy headlines for my Facebook ads promoting my [fill in the service] that helps [fill in the target audience]. Can you help?”
3. “Can you come up with compelling headlines for my Facebook ads promoting my [fill in the product/service] that is [fill in the unique selling point]?”
4. “I'm launching a new line of [fill in the product] and I need eye-catching headlines for my Facebook ads targeting [fill in the target audience]. Can you write them for me?”
5. “Can you help me write engaging headlines for my Facebook ads promoting my [fill in the product] that uses [fill in the natural/organic ingredient]?”
6. “I need headlines for my Facebook ads promoting my travel agency's [fill in the type of travel package] that offers [fill in the unique experience]. Can you assist me with that?”
7. “Can you write attention-grabbing headlines for my Facebook ads promoting my [fill in the mental health product/service] that helps [fill in the target audience] cope with [fill in the mental health issue]?”
8. “I'm running Facebook ads for my [fill in the type of book] and I need compelling headlines to attract [fill in the target audience] who want to [fill in the benefit]. Can you help me with that?”
9. “Can you generate persuasive headlines for my Facebook ads promoting my [fill in the product] that [fill in the unique selling point] and helps [fill in the benefit]?”
10. “I need headlines for my Facebook ads promoting my online store's [fill in the product category] that offers [fill in the unique selling point]. Can you write them for me?”

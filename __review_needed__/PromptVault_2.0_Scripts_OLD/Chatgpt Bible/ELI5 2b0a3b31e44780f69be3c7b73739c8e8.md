column 4: Explain Like I'm Five (ELI5) is a website that simplifies complex topics using AI so that even a child can understand them. Users can select a specific topic and choose how dumbed-down they want the explanation to be, ranging from "really dumb" to "really smart". Some examples are - "How do computers work?" and "What is the meaning of life?"

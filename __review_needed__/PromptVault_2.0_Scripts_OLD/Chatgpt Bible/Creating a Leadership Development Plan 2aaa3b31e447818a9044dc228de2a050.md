# Creating a Leadership Development Plan

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
Pretend you are a leadership development coach.
As a business owner my job is to **[brief description of the tasks you do in your business]**.
I want to create a leadership development plan. How do I identify the things that need improvement in what I am doing now?
```

```jsx
I am a business owner in the **[industry]**. I want to create a leadership development plan that allows me to **[goal]**. Give me a template of an effective leadership development plan.
```

```jsx
I want to become a better leader for my team. When I manage my team I generally **[brief description of how you manage your team]**.
What should I include in my leadership development plan?
```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "What are some effective methods for identifying my strengths and areas for improvement as a leader in a business setting?"
2. "How can I set realistic and achievable goals for my leadership development, taking into account the needs and priorities of my business?"
3. "Can you suggest some techniques for tracking progress and measuring success in my leadership development plan?"
4. "What are some ways to identify and access resources, such as training or coaching, that can support my leadership growth and development in a business context?"
5. "How can I prioritize my leadership development goals and integrate them into my overall career trajectory and business strategy?"
6. "What are some strategies for overcoming obstacles and challenges that may arise during the leadership development process?"
7. "Can you suggest some tools or software that can help with creating and implementing a leadership development plan, such as scheduling or goal tracking software?"
8. "What are some ways to hold myself accountable and maintain motivation and momentum towards my leadership development goals?"
9. "How can I leverage my leadership development plan to enhance my team management skills and create a positive and productive work environment in a business context?"
10. "What are some strategies for aligning my leadership development plan with the values and mission of my business, and incorporating them into the company culture?"

# Viral Marketing Techniques

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
My product/service appeals to **[specific audience]**. Generate a viral marketing 
campaign idea that encourages them to share with their network.
```

```jsx
I want to use **[specific platform]** to launch a viral content piece. Suggest a 
concept that leverages **[emotion, e.g., humor, nostalgia, surprise]** to maximize
engagement.
```

```jsx
Our target is to reach **[number]** shares within **[time frame]**. Write copy for a 
**[type of content, e.g., meme, video]** that inspires users to share.
```

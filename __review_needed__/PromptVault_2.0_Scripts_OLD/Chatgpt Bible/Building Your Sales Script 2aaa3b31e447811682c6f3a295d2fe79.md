# Building Your Sales Script

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
I want to write a sales script to turn my [**lead source**] leads into customers for my **[type of business]** business. What is a good process I can use?
```

```jsx
Here is the draft of a sales script I use to turn [**target audience**] into clients:
"[**insert script**]".
Can you help me improve and optimize it?
```

```jsx
My business helps **[target audience] [outcome]**. Their biggest isse at the moment is **[problem]**.
Can you write an effective sales script? Mention **[concepts you want to mention]**.
```

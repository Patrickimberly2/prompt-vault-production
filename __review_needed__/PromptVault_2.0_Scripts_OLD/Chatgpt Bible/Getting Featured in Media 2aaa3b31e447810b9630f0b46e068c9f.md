### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "Can you provide guidance on how to craft an effective press release that will catch the attention of journalists and editors?"
2. "What are some strategies for identifying and connecting with media outlets that are most likely to cover my business or industry?"
3. "How can I build relationships with journalists and media professionals to increase my chances of being featured?"
4. "Can you suggest some newsworthy angles or story ideas that could make my business more appealing to media outlets?"
5. "What should I include in a media kit, and how can I ensure that it's compelling and informative?"
6. "How can I leverage social media and other digital channels to attract the attention of journalists and media outlets?"
7. "What are some best practices for pitching my story or business to journalists, and how can I make my pitch stand out from others?"
8. "Can you suggest some creative ways to generate buzz and excitement around my business or story to attract media attention?"
9. "What are some common mistakes to avoid when trying to get featured in media, and how can I ensure that my approach is effective?"
10. "How can I measure the impact of my media coverage and use it to build my brand and attract new customers?"

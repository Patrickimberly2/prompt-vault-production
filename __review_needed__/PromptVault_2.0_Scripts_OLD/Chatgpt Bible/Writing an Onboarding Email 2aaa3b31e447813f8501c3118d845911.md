# Writing an Onboarding Email

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK **PROMPTS:**

```jsx
You are an A-list email copywriter for coaches and consultants. I am a [**niche**] coach. My [**product**] called [**name of the product**] helps [**target audience**] [**outcome**]. How can I write the onboarding email? I want them to have clarity about how the product works and make sure go through the whole [**product**] right away.
```

```jsx
Here is a welcome email: [**insert welcome email you liked**]. Can you write a new email with the same structure and angle? I am a [**niche**] coach and I help people [**outcome**]. I want new subscribers to feel happy to be on my email list and make sure they whitelist my email address.
```

```jsx
I am launching a [**product**] that helps [**audience**] with [**problem**]. The way it works is [**brief description of how it works**]. How can I explain all of this effectively in my onboarding email so that the reader has total clarity?
```

### QUESTIONS-BASED P**ROMPTS:**

1. "Can you help me create an onboarding email that effectively explains my program to new users?"
2. "What are some best practices for writing an effective onboarding email?"
3. "How can I structure my onboarding email to provide clarity and ease of use?"
4. "What should be included in an onboarding email for a new program or service?"
5. "Can you provide some tips for writing a welcoming and informative onboarding email?"
6. "What are some common mistakes to avoid in an onboarding email?"
7. "How can I use storytelling in my onboarding email to engage new users?"
8. "What tone should I use in my onboarding email to create a positive first impression?"
9. "What elements should I include in my onboarding email to encourage user engagement and retention?"
10. "What are some examples of effective onboarding emails I can use as inspiration?"

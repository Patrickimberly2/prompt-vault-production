# Writing an Order Bump Copy

### FILL-IN-THE-BLANK PROMPTS**:**

```jsx
Our **[product or service]** is the perfect solution for **[pain point]** that
many of our customers are facing. It offers **[benefit 1]**, **[benefit 2]**, and **[benefit 3]** that make it a must-have for **[target audience X]**. 
Please write a 100 word text that highlights  the product/service and encourages
customers to take action **(use urgency).**
```

```jsx
Write 3 versions of a 200 word text that compels potential customers to buy a **[type of product]** with these benefits:
**[Benefit 1]
[Benefit 2]
[Benefit 3]**
Make sure to emphasize that this is the only chance to get this product at **[price point]**, and include a call to action that says **[call to action].**
```

```jsx
Write a short landing page that offers **[product]** to **[market]**. Mention it is the only time they will see this product with a **[percentage]**% discount.
```

### OPEN-ENDED PROMPTS**:**

1. “Can you write sales copy for my new product, [product name], which is a [description of the product]? The target audience is [description of the ideal customer].”
2. “I need sales copy for my online course on [course topic]. Can you write copy that speaks to [description of the target audience] and highlights the benefits of taking the course?”
3. “I'm launching a new line of [product category]. Can you write sales copy that appeals to [description of the target audience] and emphasizes the [unique selling point] of the products?”
4. “Can you write sales copy for my e-commerce website that sells [product category]? The target audience is [description of the ideal customer] who appreciate [unique selling point of the products].”
5. “I need sales copy for my coaching services that help [target audience] achieve [goal]. Can you write copy that speaks to individuals who are [pain point of the target audience] and want to [desired outcome of the coaching services]?”
6. “I'm launching a new app that helps people manage their [problem area]. Can you write sales copy that speaks to individuals who are struggling with [problem area] and want to [desired outcome of the app]?”
7. “Can you write sales copy for my new book on [book topic]? The target audience is [description of the ideal reader] who want to [desired outcome of the book].”
8. “I need sales copy for my travel agency that specializes in [type of tourism]. Can you write copy that appeals to travelers who want to [desired outcome of the travel experience] and experience [unique selling point of the agency]?”
9. “Can you write sales copy for my online store that sells [product category]? The target audience is individuals who want to prioritize [value proposition of the products] and are passionate about [unique selling point of the products].”
10. “I'm launching a new service that helps [target audience] improve their [problem area]. Can you write sales copy that speaks to entrepreneurs who want to [desired outcome of the service] and stand out in a [description of the market]?”
